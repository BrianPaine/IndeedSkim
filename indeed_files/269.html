Opportunity at a Glance

This position is involved in the maintenance and analysis of multiple data sets.  This role reports to the Manager of Student systems and Analysis.

Responsibilities

- Collects/extracts student level data from various data sources, third party vendors, and student systems, using various software and hardware interfaces including, but not limited to, CampusVue, and SQL.
- Maintains data within the student system to ensure accurate course scheduling, program construction, student finance, and billing functions.

- Develops new data bases and data elements.
- Conducts quality checks on the completeness and accuracy of data pulled and merged into larger files.
- Presents findings of analysis in non-technical terms that can be understood by a wider audience.
- Interfaces with IT project management office and IT personnel to ensure creation institution Carrington data warehouse as per specifications.
- Merges campus-level and on-line program course schedules to create master schedule.
- Interfaces with senior leadership, including, but not limited to Academics, Student Services, Information Technology and Operations.
- Attends and participates in technical and other training.
- Assist with writing of surveys and reports and with presentation of reports.

- Completes other duties as assigned.

Qualifications

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.  Below are the minimum requirements of the education, experience, knowledge, and skills required to competently perform in this position.  Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

 

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, statistics, market research, educational research or related field required, masters preferred.
  • Minimum 2 years experience working with relational databases required, preferably in an educational setting.
  • Strong analytical skills.
  • Must have ability to present complicated information in understandable terms.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite, SPSS, Access, relational databases, SQL required.
  • Knowledge of Oracle Student System, Banner or Campus View student data platforms preferred.
  • Knowledge of and experience using web-enabled survey platforms such as Insight Express, Survey Monkey preferred.
  • Experience with Sales Force.com and Share Point preferred.
  • Ability to interact with persons of various levels in an organization.
