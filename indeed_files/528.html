We are seeking a Senior Data Analyst to join our BI team in Lewisville, TX:

Job Description

The Sr. DA is responsible for leading the process of defining system requirements, including discovery, analysis and documentation of data feeds and integrations. The ideal candidate is a self-starter with an innovative mind that can leverage their background while adapting to new technologies and methodologies, and has strong analytical, problem solving, communication skills and the ability to multi-task.

Qualifications

• Bachelor's in related field, or equivalent work experience
• 8+ years' experience in related field
• Expert in Agile development techniques
• Able to co-locate with the development team or business partners/SMEs
• Strong Data mapping and data modeling knowledge
• Strong SQL and some knowledge of SSIS would be helpful

What is expected of you and others at this level

• Applies advanced knowledge and understanding of concepts, principles, and technical capabilities to manage a wide variety of projects
• Participates in the development of policies and procedures to achieve specific goals
• Recommends new practices, processes, metrics, or models
• Works on or may lead complex projects of large scope
• Projects may have significant and long-term impact
• Provides solutions which may set precedent
• Independently determines method for completion of new projects
• Receives guidance on overall project objectives
• Acts as a mentor to less experienced colleagues
