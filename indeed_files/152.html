Data Analyst

Apply
  • Job Number: 00036619
  • Location: Haddon Heights, New Jersey
  • Organization Name: Our Lady of Lourdes Healthcare Services
  • Facility: OLLNJ - Lourdes HMSO
  • Employment Type: Full time
  • Hours: 40

The Data Analyst uses their technical experience to extract data through advanced computerized models; then, they extrapolate data patterns and trending. A working knowledge of physician billing and billing systems such as Next Gen and Intergy is required.

  • Charge trending
  • Cash reconciliation and trending
  • Payment trending to ensure correct reimbursement based on carrier contracts
  • Data extraction and analysis used for contract negotiations
  • Provides tools used for monitoring TCM and AWV
  • Addresses claim file errors related to unbilled claims
  • Runs reports used for coding and compliance audits and trending
  • Denial trending and process improvement tracking
  • Web ex training and support
  • Addresses other reporting requests in a timely manner using the Claritee Product, supporting the senior management team
  • Other duties as needed
