Working At Namaste Solar:

Namaste Solar is a 2016 Outside Magazine "Best Places to Work" award winner. Founded in 2005, Namaste Solar is a trusted designer and installer of PV solar systems for both residential and commercial customers nationwide. We do business differently. We pride ourselves on transparency (even salaries are disclosed) and giving back (company-sponsored volunteer days and donations to local charities like homeless shelters or the Humane Society).  We are committed to positive social change, and prioritize "doing the right thing" for our stakeholders. Namaste Solar is a Certified B-Corp and a WorldBlu certified democratic workplace.

 Job Description:

Namaste Solar is seeking an analytical and creative marketing professional to track, monitor, measure, analyze, and optimize the effectiveness of our content marketing initiatives as they relate to our leads and sales goals. You will manage the technical aspects of our key marketing systems (Hubspot, Salesforce) used to generate and report on key performance metrics throughout the marketing process. You will create and maintain metrics reports that deliver insights on marketing and sales activities and assess the profitability and business impact of our marketing campaigns. The Marketing Analyst will establish and maintain scalable processes that ensure best practices in campaign and lead management across multiple business units. You will manage key conversion points in the marketing funnel, including landing pages, website calls-to-action and lead generation forms. This position will conduct complex data analysis that will be used to inform strategic decisions by stakeholders from across the company.

What We're looking for:

 You'll thrive in this job if you're an analytical marketer able to turn raw metrics into actionable insights through creative and thoughtful collaboration. On top of these characteristics, we're looking for someone with:

  • Experience and proficiency with marketing automation systems (e.g. HubSpot) and integrating those systems with other technologies.
  • Strong analytical skills (including mastery of Microsoft Excel) and experience with reporting and data analysis through Google Analytics.
  • Technical capabilities and excellent communication skills with a desire to improve processes.

You don't need to have these, but you'll be even more prepared for the job if you have:

  • Proficiency with WordPress
  • Front-end web development knowledge
