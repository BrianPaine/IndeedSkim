Data Analyst
Category: Management/ Professional Schedule:
Facility: SHCN Shift:
Department: Steward Health Care Network Hours:
Req Number: 42731 Union: No
Job Details:

POSITION SUMMARY:
Reporting to the Lead of Strategic Analytics, the Data Analyst participates on a team to meet the information, reporting, and analytic needs of Steward Health Care Network (SHCN).

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

As an experienced member of the Analytics Team, the Data Analyst performs the following functions:
• Develop comprehensive, timely and accurate analyses, reports and presentations on utilization, care retention, risk performance, care management, and quality metrics on population health efforts contracts for SHCN
• Develop, compile and disseminate key reports and dashboards to SHCN leadership teams
• Provide support to key analytic initiatives by consolidating disparate data files and cleaning data so analyses and reporting is accurate
• Support ad hoc analytic requests, providing accurate and timely data, analysis and insightful interpretations
• Support senior analysts and managers with analytic projects and development of performance/projection models
• Work with SHCN staff  to assure data accuracy and integrity
• Support all necessary miscellaneous duties/tasks to support the analytic needs of the organization

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS:

• Possess strong analytic and technical skills plus an ability to translate complicated data into useable information
• Possess strong skills in Excel, Access and PowerPoint and preferably SQL
• Demonstrated knowledge of relationships between health plans and providers, including a working knowledge of health care data  (preferred, but not required)
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills, outstanding interpersonal skills and ability to relate positively with individuals at all levels of the organization
• Creative, flexible, and self-motivated with sound judgment; ability to plan and implement
• Commitment to service excellence and learning new health analytic concepts

EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE/LICENSURE/TECHNICAL/OTHER:
I. Education:  Bachelor's degree in mathematics, analytics, information systems, health administration or relevant field required
II. Experience: At least 1-2 years of analytic experience, preferably in healthcare environment
III. Certification/Licensure:  N/A
IV. Software/Hardware: SQL, Excel, Access, PowerPoint and/or other query/analytic tools
