Data Analyst
Legal

RESPONSIBILITIES: 

  • Understand business context and data elements of varying data sources
  • Collaborate with the Post Sales Surveillance team to develop and execute analytical approaches to identify potential emerging issues through data analysis and issue detection
  • Conduct data queries, text mining, and consolidation of testing, manufacturing, and post-sales data for issue analysis
  • Perform data review and analysis to identify and classify potential safety and quality issues
  • Clearly communicate analysis findings, summarize results and provide recommendations to varying levels of the organization
  • Assist in preparation for field action, Product Action Plan meetings, and status updates
  • Work with cross-functional stakeholders to gather requirements and develop reporting around relevant post-sales KPIs
  • Drive work optimization through report automation and stakeholder education
  • Execute standard and ad-hoc reports
  • Leverage organizational learnings to guide continuous improvements in data governance
  • Other duties as assigned by the needs of the department or company


QUALIFICATIONS: 
 

  • Minimum of an Associate Degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Data Analysis, or 3-5 years of equivalent work experience. Four (4) year degree preferred
  • Strong mechanical aptitude in powersports and/or automotive
  • Extensive experience in data queries, analysis, text classification and report generation
  • Strong working knowledge of MS Office applications, SharePoint, and data visualization tools
  • Excellent analytics and problem solving skills
  • Demonstrated ability to drive insights and recommendations from the data
  • Independent decision-making and resolution skills
  • Solid written and verbal communication skills and ability to work cross functionally at all levels of an organization
  • Experience with SQL for use in handling preparation and processing of large datasets
  • Working understanding of a safety and/or regulatory environment
  • Practical knowledge of Natural Language Processing and machine learning models desired
  • Off-Road Vehicle, Snowmobile, and/or Motorcycle knowledge and use preferred
