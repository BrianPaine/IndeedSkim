Excel / Data Analyst

Excel / Data Analyst
Contract Position

A client in Boston seeks an Excel / Data Analyst who has very strong data/Excel proficiency and the ability to forge strong relationships with business and IT stakeholders. A strong mindset for process improvement is also helpful for this role.
 
Responsibilities:
  • Help resolve data quality problems
  • Collaborate with business subject matter experts
  • Assess root cause of data quality issues
  • Help with addressing different errors
Qualifications:
  • Comprehends strong data quality
  • Very good Excel skills for importing and exporting
  • Very good communication skills
  • Works well with sense of urgency
Job Id: 160705
 
**Please note, our client is considering local candidates only at this time. Thank you!**
