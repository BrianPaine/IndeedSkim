US-DC

# of Openings
1

Information Technology
Work Authorization

Overview

VariQ Corporation is looking for a Data Analyst for a long-term engagement supporting our prime contract with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) with Washington, DC.

Responsibilities

  • Statistical analysis and model development using programming languages such as Python, R and Julia.
  • Work with project stakeholders and the software development team (which may include other contractors) to develop data analysis and machine learning algorithms for business stakeholders.
  • Develop analytics solutions that are scalable, repeatable, effective, and meet the expectations of the decision-makers and stakeholders.
  • Provide technical expertise for multiple advanced analytical techniques such as data mining, machine learning, geospatial analysis, natural language processing, and predictive modeling.
  • Develop creative and accurate visualizations that communicate a story, trend, pattern etc., through collaborations with other team members.
  • Incorporate data from multiple sources (relational, NoSQL, and distributed environments) into the analytics workflow by writing appropriate querying and processing code.
  • Directly support users by developing advanced reporting and analytics.
  • Assisting users in choosing data sources and discovering new methods for achieving goals.
  • Work closely with a variety of teams to identify, diagnose and resolve technical, strategic and analytical issues on behalf of business users.

Qualifications

Required Skills:

  • Significant software development experience with Python and R, with experience in using scientific libraries like numPy and SciPy.
  • Experience with Text Analysis and/or Natural Language Processing (NLP).
  • Significant experience with SQL and Database programming and ETL processing.
  • Analytics expertise with Big Data environments like Hadoop or MPP.
  • Experience working in Windows and Linux environment.
  • Communication skills and proven ability to work with customers, senior management and other technical teams
  • Documentation skills and the ability to recommend best practices and articulate process improvements and required changes
  • Organized, with the ability to meet production deadlines, and self-direct when necessary.
  • Must be Public Trust clearable

 

Desired Skills:

  • Experience creating interactive data visualizations using tools such as R Shiny, Flask, Django, or D3 and JavaScript

 

VariQ is an equal opportunity employer.
