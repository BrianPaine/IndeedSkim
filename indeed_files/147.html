Job Description

This is a re-advertisement. Applicants who have previously applied need not apply. This position is part of the Virginia Department of Health (VDH) – Office of Information Management’s (OIM) Data Management team. The purpose of this position is to provide development, systems analysis, technical support and peer troubleshooting to help improve decision making, optimize performance, and produce reports to fulfill NCHS reporting requirements. Works closely with the other data analysts in contribution to designing and supplying reports and statistics internally and other agencies that use vital event statistics to evaluate and administer health programs, and respond to legislative/regulations/mandates. Participates in development of the team’s capabilities for reliable, accurate and advanced datasets and the mechanisms to access them in safe, controlled fashion. Develops standard and ad hoc reports and data visualizations using tools such as Tableau; and evaluates user reporting requests, and provides solutions. Develops database objects, supports agency-wide data warehousing, data mart, and operational data store infrastructure; develops in SQL or PL/SQL on a regular basis; develops data visualizations and reports using tools such as Tableau; communicated with the team, supervisors, peers, data consumers and other technical staff in a professional and efficient manner; and evaluates user reporting requests, provides suggestions for a solution, and develops the solution using full application life cycle development practices and change control methodology.
Minimum Qualifications

Working experience with relational databases (Oracle preferred), SQL, PL/SQL, and/or T-SQL and spreadsheet concepts (Microsoft Excel preferred). Working experience with statistical packages (SAS, SPSS, or R). Familiar with data visualization platform such as Tableau. Good oral and written communication skills.
Preferred Qualifications

Relational database such as Oracle is preferred. Spreadsheet concept (i.e. Microsoft Excel) is preferred. A college degree with major study in statistics, mathematics or related field and/or equivalent experience and training.
Special Requirements

Employment is contingent upon satisfactory results of state and federal criminal history background check, the Department of Social Service’s Child Abuse and Neglect Central Registry check, U.S. HHS IG Exclusion List, employment reference check and E-Verify. Other financial, credit, driving, or other background checks prior to employment may be required for certain positions.
