Junior Data Analyst
Location: Washington, DC
Job Code: 468
# of openings: 1

Description

Overview:  IntelliWare is a growing small business with an immediate opening for a skilled and energetic Junior Data Analyst to support a federal client. The client is responsible for the planning, development and management of their intelligence workforce by providing integrated strategies and plans applied to source, select, allocate, train, develop, and deploy high-performance intelligence analysts for an evolving Intelligence Program. The Analyst will be part of a team to assist the client institutionalize workforce planning as well as development and management processes to support an integrated human capital lifecycle for intelligence analysts. The Analyst will support meetings, draft and review documents, correspond with Government personnel and other contractors, and provide other related services.  It is desired that the Analyst have experience working with the Intelligence Community.

Clearance:  Top Secret with SCI eligibility. Candidate will require a CI polygraph for SCI access. 

Description:

The Analyst will work in a team supporting several tasks for the client to include workforce planning and data analysis using Microsoft Excel. The Analyst will take direction from the onsite embedded Sr Human Capital Planning Analyst.

Required Skills and Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated experience in statistical analysis using Microsoft Excel and/or analyzing quantitative and qualitative data to assess program effectiveness. 

  • Experience developing surveys, conducting stakeholder interviews, and/or building assessment tools to gather information

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, including Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and SharePoint.

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.

Experience Requirements:

  • Minimum of 1-year of relevant experience.

Education Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree 

Location:  Washington, DC

Travel:  None.
