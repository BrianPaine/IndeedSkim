1752BR
Data Scientist
Fulltime-Regular
Clinical Affairs
Marlborough, MA
The Data Scientist will be responsible for all aspects of the design, development, and delivery of data-driven solutions to support the goals and objectives of Global Clincial and Health Care Economics. This includes collaborating on study parameters, data acquisition, data exploration and visualization, statistical analysis, and building the tools for these processes.

The Data Scientist will work with data from diverse structured and unstructured data sources from sponsored clinical studies, physician-initiated studies, and public and private datasets. This role will work with Global Business units, key opinion leaders, consultants, and internal teams to prepare complex data analyses and models that help deliver the significant measurable impact of AngioDynamics' products to the health care system.
 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Understand the data needs of each key product line and collaborate with Global Business Units to prioritize data collection activities.
  • Responsible for the entire analytical process, from outlining the type of solution through implementation, interpretation of results, and communicating results to stakeholders.
  • Responsible for raising awareness of good data management practices across the organization.
  • Review study protocols, with an emphasis on the review of the statistical and data management aspects of the study.
  • Review Case Report forms for compliance with accepted data standards, consistency, and efficiency.
  • Constructively discusses findings in collaboration with stakeholders while always looking for the best solution.
  • Write Statitical Analysis Plans as required.
  • Maintain SOPs related to good data management practices.
  • Create and maintain data analysis and versioning libraries.
  • Develop edit specifications to assure the accuracy of data.
  • Create validated status reports using SAS or other appropriate statistical analysis software.
  • Provide validated tables, listings, and figures for studies.
  • Create reporting mechanisims to facilitate the Adverse Event (AE) and Serious Adverse Event (SAE) reconciliation.
  • Create randomization schedules.
  • Provide sample size calculations' support to key stakeholders.
  • Provide validated reports to support US and international regulatory submission.
  • Provide training and/or insight into data management procedures and best practices at an organizational level.
  • Create data visualization tools to increase transparency and communication.
  • May perform other duties as assigned

Regulatory Responsibilities

  • Manages in order to ensure compliance with all relevant regulatory/legal requirements

QUALITY SYSTEMS DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Individual Contributor - Build Quality into all aspects of their work by maintaining compliance to all quality requirements
  • Bachelors Level of Degree in the Math, Statistics, or other analytical based field of study
  • 3+ years of demonstrated experience in analysis of complex clincial and health care data
  • SAS certification preferred
  • Experience in the following computer software applications: SAS, R, SPSS, iMedNet desired
  • Exceptional interpersonal skills.
  • Strong organizational skills.
  • Strong communication skills (written and verbal).
  • Ability to effectively communicate both internally and externally.
  • Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance, instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence.
