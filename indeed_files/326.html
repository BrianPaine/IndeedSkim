Analytics

Junior Data Analyst - Performance Lens, New Ventures

  • San Jose
Apply Now

Qualifications

  • Bachelors degree in Industrial Engineering, Finance, Statistics, Software Engineering or similar; Master's degree is a place
  • 1-3 years of experience in the analytics field
  • Experience in a multinational environment desirable
  • Advanced Excel, Visual Basic, and macro-writing knowledge
  • Great written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Sound problem-solving skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Excellent time management skills; able to lead and deliver projects on time 
  • Ability to act autonomously, bringing structure and organization to work
  • Creative and action-oriented mindset
  • Ability to work with teams across geographic time zones; comfortable participating in meetings before/after business hours

Who You'll Work With

Youll work with our Performance Lens team in San José, Costa Rica. Performance is part of McKinseys New Ventures. 

Performance Lens for Wealth & Asset Management provides fact-based, actionable insights to improve asset managers business performance by combining industry-leading data, analytics, and tools with McKinsey's deep asset management expertise.  Performance Lens can help clients to make better strategic growth and resource allocation decisions, to assess operational effectiveness, and to bring investment-like discipline to evaluating the effectiveness of sales and marketing.

McKinsey New Ventures fosters innovation driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile and social by developing new products/services and integrating them into our client work. It is helping to shift our model toward asset-based consulting and is a foundation for  and expands our investment in  our entrepreneurial culture. Through innovative software as a service solution, strategic acquisitions, and a vibrant ecosystem of alliances, we are redefining what it means to work with McKinsey.

As one of the fastest-growing parts of our firm, New Ventures has more than 1,500 dedicated professionals (including more than 800 analysts and data scientists) and were hiring more mathematicians, data scientists, designers, software engineers, product managers, client development managers and general managers.

What You'll Do

You'll be responsible for the Excel-based analytics models, report production model, and data mapping to our online web applications.

In this role you will focus on the analytics behind a variety of global benchmarking efforts, including the North America Asset Management Survey and Insurance General Account Survey. In order to suceed, you will need to be analytically-driven, with strong excel modeling, digital analytics, and statistical modeling.

You'll have the opportunity to work with McKinsey colleagues from around the world and liaise directly with external clients. You will support McKinsey consultants by preparing complex data analysis that will help solve client problems and deliver significant measurable impact.
