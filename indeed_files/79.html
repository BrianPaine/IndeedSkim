Develops and manages policies and procedures relating to the entry, storage, maintenance, and security of personal and demographic data relating to students, staff, and affiliates of the university in all corporate systems.
  • Develops and manages policies and procedures relating to the entry, storage, maintenance, and security of personal and demographic data relating to students, staff, and affiliates of the university in all corporate systems.
  •  Works with functional areas to develop policies, standards, definitions, and conventions governing the creation and use of enterprise bio/demo data.
  •  Implements system administration procedures such as data validation processes, duplicate resolution processes, system change requests and system integration / user acceptance testing, to ensure ongoing integrity and security of people data in line with university policy and procedure.
  • Establishes definitions, standards, and naming conventions for enterprise administrative data to ensure ongoing integrity, accuracy, and internal consistency of administrative data.
  •  Prepares and disseminates reference sources containing pertinent policies, definitions, and other information necessary to use administrative data.
  • Audits data and reports results to functional areas for reinforcement of established policies, standards, definitions, and conventions.
  •  Supports the applicable systems and databases for access, error resolution, and processes to keep data in sync with a variety of inter-related systems.
  •  Coordinates testing of new releases of the application to identify impacts to and across data libraries and/or databases to ensure data integrity.
Work Schedule: Monday – Friday, 8 am – 5 pm with extended hours as needed
