Analyst, Risk I - 506620

Organization: Financial Services

Department: Enterprise Risk Management

Location: Knoxville, Tennessee

Posting Open: 10/02/2017

Posting Close: 10/09/2017 at 11:59PM EST

Grade: 02

 

POSITION PURPOSE:

Serves as a junior financial analyst providing economic, financial and risk management expertise related to power resource and supply investment to all areas of TVA.  Participates in cross-organizational project teams in the development and implementation of major financial, risk management and business development projects.

 

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:

Under direction/supervision of senior staff members, the incumbent:

  • Performs operational risk and financial analyses(valuation, risk, and decision analysis) of new power plant (gas, nuclear, coal) and power purchase opportunities, and existing TVA generation assets.
  • Assists in the development and analysis of power related business opportunities with inter-departmental teams.  Produces formal results and presentations.
  •  Develops computer models for valuation, including mark-to-market, portfolio, real options, and other methods as needed.
  • Performs financial and risk analyses for the benefit of other TVA organizations.  This includes the development of applicable financial evaluation procedures and models, field evaluations, and final recommendations to the manager of the applicable organization.
  • Serves on agency-wide projects that have been established to resolve key business issues.

 

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

Education –Bachelor’s degree in finance, business, econmics or related field is required.  An advanced degree in Business or Finance is desirable.

Experience  – 0-3 years of experience.

Knowledge/Skills/Abilities —Must have basic knowledge of the principles of business, economics, finance, and financial accounting.  Requires technical knowledge and ability to analyze competing financial proposals and must possess excellent oral and written communication skills.  Must be proficient in the use of spreadsheets, word processing, data analysis and graphics.  Candidate may be required to obtain and maintain a security clearance based on position / access requirements and essential job functions.
