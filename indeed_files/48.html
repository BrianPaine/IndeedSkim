Position Details

Savings Institute Bank & Trust welcomes friendly professionals who are dedicated to providing superior customer service and helping our customers find the right solution.

Data Analyst

North Windham, CT

Savings Institute Bank & Trust Company has an immediate opening for a Data Analyst in its IT Department located in North Windham, CT.

Responsibilities include:

  • SQL SSMS Query Design and Data Mining
  • Efficiencies using Ad-Hoc reporting
  • Document Imaging
  • Electronic Records Retention
  • Administration of the Technology Oversight Program
  • Provide quality control over all Information Technology controls
  • End User Productivity
  • End User Satisfaction
  • Optimum Technology Performance

    • Successful candidates will have:
  • High School Diploma
  • Experience utilizing database reporting software such as Crystal Reports, SQL Server Reporting Services, Business Objects Enterprise tools or other report writing tools.
  • Experience handling documents digitally
  • Comparable combination of education and experience.
  • Ability to operate office equipment
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word), Outlook Email, and Adobe *.pdf documents
  • Experience with HelpDesk administration software

    Benefits include: Competitive pay, 401K, paid vacations and holidays, health/dental insurance, tuition reimbursement and bonuses.
