Order Status

Generally, the best way to check the status of your order is to contact customer service. To contact Customer Service call 1.800.837.4235, or fill out our customer service form.

HIGH SCHOOL CLASS RINGS:

Class rings are custom made just for you! Rings generally are delivered 8-12 weeks after ordering takes place in school.

HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATION PRODUCTS:

Graduation announcements and other senior products purchased in school are either provided at the time of purchase or are commonly delivered in the February to April timeframe. Caps and gowns are typically delivered very close to graduation. Specific details regarding delivery will be communicated through your school.

Go Back
COLLEGE RINGS

College rings are delivered 8-12 weeks after the order has been placed. All rings shipping to a residential or business address will be shipped via FedEx. All rings shipping to a PO Box or General Delivery will be shipped via the U.S. Postal Service (USPS).

Go Back
COLLEGE GRADUATION PRODUCTS

Caps & Gowns may be distributed on campus or shipped to you directly, depending on your school’s preference and your selection at time of order. Bulk distribution dates will be communicated through your school. For direct ship orders, please allow 2-3 weeks for manufacturing. Once your order ships, a shipment confirmation email including the tracking number will be sent to the email address provided. Graduation Announcements are shipped directly to the address listed at time of order. Graduation announcements should arrive within 12 – 15 business days.

Go Back
YEARBOOKS

Unless your school offers a home mailing option and you have paid for that separately, distribution will occur on campus at a time scheduled and announced by your school.
