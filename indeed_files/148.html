Analyst (Entry-Level)

Insurance Edison, New Jersey

Apply

Description

COMPANY DESCRIPTION:
Foresters Financial is a financial services firm with administrative and operations offices located in Edison, NJ and on Wall Street as well as regional sales offices located nationwide.

 

POSITION SUMMARY:

Under the guidance of the Model Office supervisor, assist in the management of projects related to the life insurance systems.  Work with appropriate supervision to complete plan file testing, product conversions, data scrubbing and system troubleshooting.  Additional functions include acting as a liaison with the vendor (LIDP), IT support and mainframe operations.  The incumbent would possess a detailed understanding of FLIAC’s various departments, systems, workflows, functions and products.

 

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Assist in the development of the various test plans for conversions.
  • Assist in new product development by understanding the plan file capabilities.
  • Implement test plans by processing various transactions during conversion.
  • Compute/review data manually to ensure the accuracy of products.
  • Collaborate with the vendor (LIDP), IT staff and mainframe operations on problem resolution (i.e., response time, printing, interfaces and system upgrades).
  • Resolve system issues that occur on the interfaces in all departments.
  • Act as training and troubleshooting resource for the company’s administrative system issues.
  • Provide input to develop training procedures for new products converted onto the system.
  • Liaise between the various service departments to gather ideas on improving or upgrading the administrative system.

 

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s degree required (Mathematics majors preferred)

 

EXPERIENCE AND BACKGROUND REQUIREMENTS:

  • Insurance experience a plus; System conversion experience a plus
  • Proficient in Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel with accurate data entry skills; Access a plus
  • Strong quantitative, analytical and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work effectively in a project management environment
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Detail-oriented with excellent organizational skills

 

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: 

  • Lifting stacks of reports and boxes up to 40 lbs.
