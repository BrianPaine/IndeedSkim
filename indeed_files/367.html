    Application Insights
    Data Insights Analyst
    Location: Hermitage/Nashville, TN
     
    About the team
    The Application Insights team serve our customers—the business and technical teams—with data-driven insights to guide data-focused, informed business and investment decisions and solve business challenges. The team prioritizes accelerating user adoption and measuring the success of applications. Their competencies range from establishing and maintaining client relationships, understanding client desired outcomes, setting performance metrics sets, defining KPI’s, establishing analytics tracks, to preparing data for analytic purposes, performing statistical analysis, creating data visualizations, and being able to develop insights and present them to different audiences through diverse mediums.
     
    Role Description
    The Data Insights Analyst will gather client requirements, collect, combine, visualize and analyze data from disparate sources to deliver relevant and actionable statistics and insights for business decision support.  The success of this candidate is predicated on providing insights that enable owners and stakeholders to:
    • Gauge productivity and efficiency gains on developed applications and product features (where to invest further, or reduce investment)
    • Leverage data assets and create outcome focused insights for decision support
    • Provide Change Management teams with insight on where to focus their efforts to improve efficiencies
     
    Job responsibilities:
    • Access, clean, store, visualize, analyze, and interpret raw data culled from various data sources available for final consumption by clients of differing levels of technical/business understanding and need, as appropriate.
    • Create relevant and visually compelling data visualizations that incorporate advanced filtering and functionality for optimal data interaction for analysts and business users.
    • Identify key application features, define and deliver KPIs for business applications.
    • Analyze customer usage data, identify patterns and derive insights for application and client or portfolio success.
    • Correlate different data points and attributes to identify clusters of opportunity for business process improvement, practitioner productivity gains, cost savings or analytic insight.
    • Transform data points and audience measurement into compelling insights in clearly distilled and displayed deliverables that effectively communicate the relevant and reliable messages.
    • Independently manage project deadlines to ensure the timely delivery of requested insights.
    • Contribute ideas and assist in cultivating concepts during team brainstorm gatherings, working on special projects and partnering with peers and other data departments.

    • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Statistics, Information Systems, Computer Science, Statistics, Data Curation or related field
    • 3 plus years of experience in IT related field including business analytics experience, research and insights
    • Experience Analytics Tools: Tableau, SSMS, SPSS, Data mining software (such as QDA Miner, Rapid Miner, etc.), ETL: MS Visual Studio or Alteryx
    • Experience with Adobe Digital Analytics (Site Catalyst/Omniture), or other digital analytics tool
    • Possess excellent verbal and written communication skills
    • Ability to juggle multiple projects and prioritize accordingly in a deadline driven environment
    • Motivated self-starter who has a strong desire and curiosity to learn about data science, advanced analytics and statistical methods
    • Resourceful problem-solver with a willingness and determination to find and tell the most compelling story
    • Ambition to deliver exceptional and impactful insights that deliver desired outcomes
    • Extremely attentive to detail
    • Organized and diligent with strong time management skills
    • Proficient in Microsoft Office, specifically PowerPoint and Excel Data Analysis Pack
