Operations Support Analyst 

Agility 360 is looking to fill the position of Operations Support Analyst for a well-established client in the Dallas/Ft. Worth area.  This position will be Originations focused and conduct research, compile findings, and offer support to various areas of the business including quality control & compliance.  The ideal candidate will successfully collaborate with management in the build-out of the disclosure process, as well as the preparation and response during audit requests.  Continued support and improvement of the company's originations platform including internal and regulatory research, documentation, and promotion will be a priority.  This person will have a solid understanding of mortgage originations with bench strength in writing procedures, project management, and analytical support.

What you will do...
  • Partner with multiple departments to design and execute on various projects
  • Establish a process for timely and compliant delivery of disclosures
  • Research and write clear and accurate responses during the review process
  • Conduct all preparation for upcoming audits
  • Organize and archive all data points for streamlined access
What you need to have...
  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 3 years mortgage origination experience
  • Minimum 5 years in policy/procedure or technical writing
  • Experience working with tools including Adobe RoboHelp
Skills we are looking for...
  • Excellent communication both written and verbal
  • Ability to prioritize projects and quickly adjust as needed
  • Strong background in mortgage origination regulatory environment
Details...
  • Position will office in the Dallas area with standard business hours
  • Role does not have direct reports
  • Salary will range from $55k-$65k + benefits
