Systems & Reporting Analyst - Associate Job

Full Job Title: Systems and Rptg Analyst, Assoc

Job Number: 1702657

Location: Tampa, FL

Date Posted: 10-2-2017

Apply Now
LOCATION: George Road, Tampa, FL
DEPARTMENT: Finance
REPORTING TO: Senior Manager, Pharmacy Analytics
The Associate will run assigned standardized processes while learning the data, table structures and the business. Associates will be assigned a technical "mentor" within the Team, at a Senior or Lead level, for reference/guidance and will be assigned projects of increasing complexity as skill and knowledge develop.
Essential Functions:
  • Running standardized processes per requirements, reviewing/QA'ing results, and distributing as needed.
  • Develop an understanding of the assigned processes sufficient to modify/update code upon request.
  • Test process documentation against the current code, updating as needed for accuracy and clarity.
  • Create Ad Hoc reports, queries and/or analyses upon request.
  • Report analytics and interpretations of data anomalies as data knowledge develops.
Education:
  • Bachelor's Degree is preferred OR
  • High school diploma or equivalent, plus relevant technical programming certification/technical training equivalent to two (2) years OR
  • High school diploma or equivalent, plus two (2) years relevant professional/technical experience and above average performance history
Experience:
  • Two (2) plus years' relevant healthcare experience AND
  • One (1) plus years' relevant programming and/or analytical experience
Licenses/Certifications: SAS Certification is a plus
Special Skills (e.g. 2nd language): NA
Technical Skills/Requirements:
  • Basic skills/experience in relational database querying.
  • Basic skills/experience in Access, Visio and PowerPoint required.
  • Advanced MS Excel skills required.
