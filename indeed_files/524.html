Job Title: Business Development Analyst

Department: Strategic Accounts

Reports to: Director of Sales Operations

Direct reports:  0         

Indirect reports: 0

FLSA Status: Exempt              

EEO: 2

 

Job Summary:

Wencor Group is looking for a passionate certified Business Development Analyst. The successful candidate will turn data into information, information into insight and insight into business development decisions.

Responsibilities include conducting full lifecycle analysis to include requirements, activities and design. This role will develop analysis and reporting capabilities, and will also monitor performance and quality control plans to identify improvements.

Examples of Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports
  • Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
  • Partnering with Strategic Development Program Manager to develop solid business solutions for the major companies within the  aviation/aerospace industry
  • Acquire and analyze industry and market data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
  • Filter and "clean" data by reviewing computer reports, printouts, and performance indicators to locate and correct code problems
  • Work with management to prioritize business and information needs
  • Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
  • Prepare customer and internal presentations explaining the solutions approach
  • Prepare commercial & financial analysis (business cases) to understand ROI or other financial KPIs
  • Prepare commercial & legal framework in cooperation with offer / proposal management / legal departments
  • Coordinate internally all required activities to develop solution
  • Support team leader with completed account plans and relationship model understanding

Qualifications and Skills.

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.  Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • Proven working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst
  • Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques
  • Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (Business Objects etc), databases (SQL etc), programming (XML, Javascript, or ETL frameworks)
  • Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc)
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Adept at queries, report writing and presenting findings

Education Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering (preferred), Marketing, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics
  • Three years of related experience and/or training; or
  • Equivalent combination of education and experience.
