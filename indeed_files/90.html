Data Analyst

Location
Atlanta, United States
Job Type
Information Analyst
Category
Full Time

Description

Team Daugherty is hiring Data Analysts to join us in the Atlanta office. The ideal candidates for this position are problem solvers with the ability to utilize insights, creativity and perspective to drive business success for our clients.

As a Data Analyst you will have the opportunity to work on:

  • Architecture and integration of data warehouse combining many data sources into one coherent model for enterprise reporting.
  • Common integration technologies: SSIS, Informatica, Datastage.
  • Developing enterprise reporting strategies for clients with multiple disparate point of sale systems.
  • Creating reports and dashboards for varying levels of the organization (operational through executive management).
  • Common reporting technologies: SAP Business Objects, SSRS, Microstrategy, Tableau, Qlikview.
  • Designing data models and data mapping that adhere to data architecture standards and strategies, and follow best practices.
  • Common database technologies: Teradata, Oracle, and SQL Server.
  • Elicitation of business requirements: meet with clients to gain an understanding of their business needs using Daughertys BI-RPM Methodology.
  • Creating robust project estimates using Daughertys proven BI Dimensional Estimator.
  • Leverage a deep network of experienced information consultants that can support you along the way.

We are looking for someone with:

  • 3-5 years of relevant experience with a focus on data management, data analytics, visualization and process analysis.
  • Programming skills with Python, R, Matlab, C, C++, Java are a plus.
  • A strong background in data science and machine learning.
  • A good understanding of statistical techniques and applying the principles in solving complex business problems.
  • Demonstrated expertise in advanced analytics skills and the cloud.
  • 3+ years of experience supporting the development of analytics solutions.
  • A high level of comfort narrating data-driven insights and translating technical concepts into simple terminology for a variety of technical and non-technical stakeholders at various levels.
  • Excellent problem solving skills, including the ability to analyze situations, identify existing or potential problems, and recommend sound conclusions and implementation strategies.
  • Demonstrated knowledge, experience and understanding of enterprise data concepts like data warehousing, handling large data volume, and using multiple tools to research and analyze information.
  • Professional development experience with, and knowledge of, relational databases, enterprise data warehouses, and cloud-based web services.
  • Knowledge of best practices and principles for data modeling, dashboards, report design, analytics, and data mining.
  • Experience with big data technology (Hadoop, Apache Spark, MapReduce, etc.) is a plus.

We offer members of Team Daugherty:

  • Excellent health, dental and vision insurance.
  • Revenue sharing and a 401(k) retirement savings plan.
  • Life, disability and long-term care insurance.
  • Little to no travel.
  • Robust career development and training.
