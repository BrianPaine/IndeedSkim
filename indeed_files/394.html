Purpose and Scope of Position:

The Data Analyst / SQL Report Writer oversees enhancing the Internal Customer Experience by producing workable reports and KPIs to be used throughout the manufacturing process. This position will also be responsible for developing custom analytical products that help provide key data capabilities and insights for multiple stakeholders across our administrative and production teams. This position requires an organized individual with a love for data integrity and data structure.

 

Key Responsibilities:

  • Candidates must have three or more years of experience in MSSQL reporting
  • Developing complex MSSQL scripts, views, and stored procedures
  • Understand the needs of the internal customer beyond what is initially expressed
  • Understanding of a wide range of interpretive analysis tools (e.g. trend analysis, spreadsheets, comparisons, graphs, summary versus detail charts, drill down, correlations, exception analysis)
  • Developing reports in MSSQL, and other reporting tools such as Power BI, Access, and Excel.
  • Ensure data is clean and correct in the data warehouse and live systems and report any discrepancies
  • Automate or increase efficiency of existing reports
  • SQL tuning & data profiling
  • Ability to question and eyeball data quality in reports.
  • Analytical, problem-solving and conceptual skills.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills and the ability to communicate effectively with non-technical people
  • Support the internal customers to ensure timely and accurate delivery of data

 

 

Required Experience

3 years in MSSQL Report writing

 

Language Requirements

English Required Only

 

Education and Training Requirements:

At least 3 years' experience in MSSQL report writing.

BS degree preferred.
