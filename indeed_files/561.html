Job title: Business Data Analyst

Corporate Title: Analyst

Location: New York, NY

Business Unit: Operations

 

Company overview

Nomura is an Asia-based financial services group with an integrated global network spanning over 30 countries. By connecting markets East & West, Nomura services the needs of individuals, institutions, corporates and governments through its three business divisions: Retail, Asset Management, and Wholesale (Global Markets and Investment Banking). Founded in 1925, the firm is built on a tradition of disciplined entrepreneurship, serving clients with creative solutions and considered thought leadership. For further information about Nomura, visit www.nomura.com

 

Department Overview:

This position will be part of the Corporate Business Intelligence Team, specifically reporting into the US Metrics Manager. The CBIT team is the initial contact within Operations for inquiries related to key risk and performance indicators. The team is responsible for delivering over 100 regional reports each day to hundreds of internal clients. Team members should be able to bring both technical skills and people skills to be able to interact across cultures with global colleagues.

 

Role description:

         The position will report into the CBIT Manager and will primarily support the Operations business.

         Responsible for supporting ongoing maintenance of existing reports and datamarts as well as new report development.

         Develop and customize SAP Business Objects reports.

         Troubleshoot and increase efficiency of existing Business Objects Reports.

         Work with clients and stakeholders across Compliance, Audit, Control and Middle Office to understand business requirements

         Document requirements and data needs appropriately

Skills, experience, qualifications and knowledge required:

         2+ years of Financial Services industry experience, with a good understanding of the terminology and processes.

         Strong excel and PowerPoint skills

         Experience working with and building relationships with internal stakeholders including IT staff

         Strong communication skills in English, both written and verbal.

         Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.

         Experience writing Business Requirements documents preferred

         Experience with SAP Business Objects or other Business Intelligence tools preferred

         Experience with SQL preferred
