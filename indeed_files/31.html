Data Analyst I

Location: Atlanta, Georgia Job Number: 1716095 Brand: Cox Communications

Description

The Consumer Analytics group in Marketing at Cox Communications, Inc. is looking for a Data Analyst I who is incredibly passionate about data, who has a proven track record of transforming raw data into analytic insight, and who desires to make a difference by leveraging the right techniques to arrive at the right answers. 

Your primary role will be to work on high-impact business problems and develop viable solutions through data analysis, predictive modeling, and advanced analytics techniques.  The candidate should have a combination of excellent analytic skill, strong business acumen, and excellent interpersonal and communication skills. They will work closely with other Analytics team members in Marketing, Product, Operations, and Technology.  This is a great opportunity to be part of an analytics team that does leading-edge work in a great organization.

Primary Roles and Responsibilities

  • Independently execute analytical projects supporting decisions on revenue, products and customer metrics.
  • Develop innovative, intuitive, and data-driven methods for measuring program effectiveness
  • Conduct statistical and quantitative analyses including multivariate regression, segmentation, clustering, and pricing/demand models
  • Extract and clean data from multiple data sources of any size
  • Translate business problems into analytical problems.  Develop analytical solutions including metrics, methodology, and output. Infer business meaning of results to provide recommendations to stakeholders.
  • Develop effective presentations to communicate results to business leaders.
  • Facilitate the production of dashboards, complex models and analyses through code.
  • Support development of complex analytical models and analytical projects, moving them towards successful completion.

Qualifications

Minimum

  • 1+ year of experience using SAS for modeling and analysis and/or SQL for data analysis
  • Excellent data extraction and manipulation skills using standard tools (SAS, SQL, or similar)
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

Preferred

  • BS/BA in quantitative field strongly desired.
  • Experience with Econometric modeling and Machine Learning methodology
  • Experience with customer lifecycle management, customer segmentation and customer lifetime value scoring
