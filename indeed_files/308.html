Database Analyst

Albuquerque, New Mexico, United States · Finance

Description

Join a dynamic, fast-paced entrepreneurial organization that is in the solar industry! Unirac is a market leader in solar racking solutions. We offer a healthy work environment, alternative work schedules, great people to work with, affordable benefits with a fun wellness program and attractive paid time off bank.

We are seeking an enthusiastic, positive Enterprise Resource Planning Coordinator/Analyst. This position performs three different functions include system administration, financial analysis and some supporting accounting duties. System administration is performed with GlobalShop, Sale Force and CodeSoft. Financial analysis will include utilizing Crystal reporting, Excel and Access. This position will also serve as a back-up in accounting processes at month end and with some AP and AR functions. This position is located in downtown Albuquerque.

Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • Cover letter with resume submission
  • Associates or Bachelor’s degree in math, finance or statistics or 5+ comparable years of experience
  • Must possess 3-5 years of experience as an analyst
  • Must possess experience with data mining and converting it to useful information through reporting
  • Must have advanced skill level in Excel and Access
  • Must have ability to work with all levels of management/employees
  • Must possess high level of attention to detail
  • Must have basic accounting skills
  • Must possess experience with database management
  • Must have strong analytical skills


Preferred Requirements:

  • Prior experience in manufacturing environment
  • Prior experience with writing reporting’s using Crystal Reporting
  • Prior experience in solar


Job Competencies:

  • Knowledge – Basic accounting and reconciliation practices
  • Knowledge – Financial analysis techniques and basic math skills
  • Skill – Database applications maintenance and reporting
  • Skill – Data gathering, interpretive and qualitative analysis, tracking and reporting
  • Skill – Time management to include prioritizing and reprioritizing, adaptable scheduling and flexibility and the handling of multiple tasks
  • Ability - To be organized in approach, be flexible and alter priorities when needed
  • Ability – To build rapport, influence and utilize diplomacy and tact at all levels
  • Ability – To ask questions, be self-motivated and self-directed and possess a willingness to learn
  • Ability – To learn, retain and apply knowledge to various situations
  • Ability – To be detail oriented, anticipate next steps and follow through to completion

Ideal Candidate:

Ideal candidate will meet all the minimum requirements and some of the preferred requirements. Candidate will have a great can-do attitude, be a team player and be able to communicate with all levels of management and all types of people. Candidate will possess good analytical skills, have a high attention to detail and have an ability to learn new processes and applications quickly. The ideal will also have a strong technical skill set and have had experience working with databases, possess advanced skills in Excel and Access. Candidates will also have had finance background with some accounting experience. Candidates will be flexible and be comfortable with change and be comfortable with trying new things and making suggestions where needed.
