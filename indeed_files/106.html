Job Details

Req No. 17-0926
Title Data Analyst
Location BAMSI Administration
City Brockton
Scheduled Hours 40
Company Info

        JOIN OUR TEAM OF DEDICATED PROFESSIONALS!

The Data Analyst at BAMSI willl provide strategic and tactical support by collecting, organizing, analyzing and interpreting agency data to facilitate self-assessment and quality improvement.

BAMSI is a 1900+ employee health and human services organization with locations across Eastern Massachusetts. We offer generous paid time off, paid holidays, paid trainings in a supportive environment that offers growth opportunities! Health and Dental Insurance is available for employees working 30+ hours.

Bachelors degree, preferably in a relevant discipline .Minimum 5 years of experience in a position focused on the delivery of numerical data to a wide variety of customers. Experience in health care strongly preferred. Advanced proficiency use of desktop productivity tools including Excel, Word, PowerPoint. Demonstrated ability to use Access, SQL or similar analytic tools. Ability to organize, synthesize and analyze large amounts of information. Ability to manage multiple projects and deliver required products in a timely manner. Must be well-organized and detail-oriented with a commitment to producing analytic product that consistently meets the highest standards of accuracy.
Description

Essential Functions:

1.   Interpret data, analyze results using basic statistical techniques and produce and present reports as requested.

2.   Develop and implement data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and data quality.

3.   Work with colleagues and customers of Quality and Improvement to obtain and understand internal and external data sources.

4.   Increase the content and value of Quality and Improvement reporting, based upon agency requirements and employing industry standards and best practices.

5.   Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems.

6.   Assure the integrity of Quality and Improvement data, including data extraction, storage, manipulation, processing and analysis.

7.   Monitor Virtual Gateway alerts and status changes and perform related work.

8.   Work closely with management to prioritize business and information needs.

9.   Identify and define process improvement opportunities; document workflow.

10. Work with Quality and Improvement colleagues to analyze incident and investigation data for trends and patterns.

11. Work collaboratively with other BAMSI departments as needed to accomplish objectives.

12. Participate on the Quality and Improvement Committee, MA Virtual Gateway HCSIS workgroups and others as assigned.

13. Travel by personal automobile to perform duties and attend trainings/meetings as required.

14. Perform duties in compliance with applicable laws and regulations, agency policies and procedures

(including safety standards and training requirements) and industry best practices.

Non-Essential Functions:

1. Perform other work-related duties as needed or as assigned by supervisor or designee.
Requirements

Qualifications:

1.   Bachelors degree, preferably in a relevant discipline.
2.   Minimum 5 years of experience in a position focused on the delivery of numerical data to a wide variety of customers.
3.   Experience in health care strongly preferred.
4.   Advanced proficiency use of desktop productivity tools including Excel, Word, PowerPoint.
5.   Demonstrated ability to use Access, SQL or similar analytic tools.
6.   Ability to organize, synthesize and analyze large amounts of information.
7.   Ability to manage multiple projects and deliver required products in a timely manner.
8.   Must be well-organized and detail-oriented with a commitment to producing analytic product that consistently meets the highest standards of accuracy.
9.   Ability and willingness to understand and meet the information needs of wide variety of internal customers of Quality and Improvement.

 

10. Excellent interpersonal skills and the demonstrated ability to work in a team oriented environment.

11. Must be able to communicate effectively verbally and in writing with diverse audience, including technical and non-technical colleagues, funders, external agencies and other agency stakeholders.

12. Ability to explain data analysis to non-technical audiences.

13. Maintains absolute confidentiality of all agency data and other materials.

14. Must be highly self-motivated and able to manage competing priorities and meet deadlines.

15. Must possess a valid drivers license in state of residence, a good driving record, and own means of reliable automobile transportation.

16. Ability to pass a background check, including a criminal background check.
