Data Analyst

Analytics Oakland, California

Description

We are actively seeking a Data Analyst to drive strategic analyses, sales planning, and metrics reporting for our Sales, Marketing, and Business Development teams.  The Data Analyst role will be cross-functional and interact with key stakeholders throughout Renew Financial.  Our ideal candidate would have a solid quantitative background, excellent attention to detail, the ability to work independently, and an imaginative curiosity about how to leverage data.

The Data Analyst role will be an individual contributor position, reporting to the Director of Analytics.  

Responsibilities:

  • Design and prepare weekly/monthly reporting on sales volume, key trends, and operational metrics for executive management and sales teams
  • Develop forecast models on sales trends, business drivers, and business cases related to key sales, marketing, and business development programs
  • Create business cases and scoring system to optimize sales and marketing efforts on customers, sales prospects, and potential new opportunities
  • Build and automate dashboard reports on sales and operating results
  • Partner with Finance and Sales Operations teams on financial planning tools, sales compensation reporting, and budgeting/planning
  • Become trusted analytics partner across the company, providing valuable source of data-based insights
  • Support Sales and Marketing teams on ad-hoc statistical and financial analyses

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Economics, Finance, Statistics, or equivalent
  • 2-3 years of prior work experience in analytics, corporate finance, FP&A, or consulting function
  • Excellent quantitative and problem solving skills
  • Self-starter with initiative to partner collaboratively across the organization
  • Power user in Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Experience using and managing Salesforce
  • Proficient knowledge of statistical query languages (R; SQL; Python) preferred
