Description:

The Leidos Health Products & Solutions Operation has an opening for a Mid Level Data Analyst with experience in the commercial healthcare or related industry to support the design, integration, testing and delivery of the Leidos healthcare enterprise platform.

Responsible for collecting, interpreting, and analyzing statistical information. Duties include using statistical software (SAS, SPSS, etc.) to create and maintain tabulations, graphical summaries and formal statistical estimates and tests.
Develops solutions to complex technical issues and problems that impact multiple area or disciplines.
Regularly employs ingenuity and creativity to develop new technical solutions and systems in order to achieve functional objectives.
Effectively communicates with internal team members across multiple areas and client team members.
May communicate with parties external to the organization (e.g., sub-contractors, vendors, etc.).
Works to influence project/team leaders regarding solution design, process and/or approaches.
Work requires expert knowledge of and ability to apply advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts in doing data analytics.


Qualifications:


Required Qualifications:
• Bachelor's degree and a min of 8 + year's of relevant experience
• Demonstrated ability to rapidly understand and solve problems in new subject areas by applying statistical techniques.
• Demonstrated ability to work with stakeholders to conceptualize, prototype, develop, and demonstrate data analytic solutions.
• Ability to develop complex data visualizations, predictive analytics, and apply techniques such as machine learning to deliver solutions.
• Expertise in statistical analytic tools such as R, SAS, SPSS.
• Experience with analytic data preparation methods, including data profiling, data quality, data transformation
• Knowledge of new and developing “big data” processing mechanisms, including Hadoop.
• Ability to work in the context of a team based environment and typical software development lifecycle processes.

Desired Qualifications:
• Experience with tools for data visualization & exploration, machine learning, link analysis
• Experience with training end users on tools and techniques
• Domain knowledge of various subject areas such as manufacturing, sales & marketing, finance

 

*Summer referral eligible


Leidos Overview:
Leidos is a global science and technology solutions leader working to solve the world’s toughest challenges in the defense, intelligence, homeland security, civil, and health markets. The company’s 33,000 employees support vital missions for government and commercial customers. Headquartered in Reston, Virginia, Leidos reported pro forma annual revenues of approximately $10 billion for the fiscal year ended January 1, 2016 after giving effect to the recently completed combination of Leidos with Lockheed Martin's Information Systems & Global Solutions business (IS&GS). For more information, visit www.Leidos.com. The company’s diverse employees support vital missions for government and commercial customers. Qualified women, minorities, individuals with disabilities and protected veterans are encouraged to apply.
