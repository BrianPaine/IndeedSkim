Data Analyst

Department: General & Admin
Location: Novi, MI
START YOUR APPLICATION

SUMMARY:  The Data Analyst collects and analyzes data to be used for reporting on the various business practices at CST.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Provide reporting, analysis and insightful recommendations to the management team on performance metrics
  • Write and maintain SQL queries, stored procedures, and views
  • Generate standard or custom reports summarizing business, financial, or patient statistics for review by executives, managers, and clients
  • Collaborate closely with managers to prioritize and identify key issues that, when solved, will help grow orders, sales and profit
  • Collaborate with end users to identify needs and opportunities for improved data management and delivery
  • Quality check reports of other data analysts to guarantee integrity of data and ensure queries are not strenuous to our database
  • Using SQL, perform mass inserts, and updates to our database
  • Analyze processes throughout the departments and identify possible inefficiencies
  • Participate in an on-call schedule rotation
  • All other tasks as assigned

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems or equivalent work experience
  • Proficiency in Reporting Software such as Crystal Reports
  • Understanding of rational databases
  • Proficiency in Standard Query Language (SQL)
  • Proficient with ODBC connections
  • Intermediate to advance skills in Microsoft Excel
  • Excellent organizational skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Displays exceptional professionalism
  • Commitment to excellent customer service
  • Excellent problem solving and analytical skills
  • Ability to work independently with little supervision
