Business Data Analyst - Generalist

Apply now

Department:

JOB DESCRIPTION

The Data Analyst is an analyst position with the E15 Group. As a Business Data Analyst - Generalist, you would become a primary resource supporting projects across multiple industries, spanning a client base that includes the world’s most recognizable sports franchises such as the Chicago Cubs and Golden State Warriors to global tech giants such as Amazon and Google. In this role you would contribute on project teams focused on leveraging advanced analytical methods to deliver insights in the areas of business intelligence, business optimization, and consumer behavior.

Some of the core responsibilities of the Business Data Analyst - Generalist are:

  • Analyzing data (from a variety of sources) to answer business questions and optimize our clients’ businesses
  • Managing various workflows relating to projects for key client and internal stakeholders as well as understanding their overall strategies and business objectives
  • Identifying opportunities to change and improve existing methods of analysis and reporting
  • Identifying opportunities to change and improve existing processes to capture and leverage data and institutionalize learning
  • Supporting the periodic reporting on operational and financial metrics for specific clients, and creating an economic valuation of E15’s bottom-line impact
  • Supporting the periodic reporting and analysis of consumer insights data (surveys, syndicated data, focus groups, etc.)
  • Managing or directly completing ad hoc analytics projects as appropriate

REQUIRED SKILLS
  • Bachelor’s (or higher) degree in business analytics, computer science, data science, statistics, economics, finance, psychology, marketing, consumer behavior, or a related field
  • Experience (work or school assignments) in at least one of the following tools:
    • SQL
    • Statistical software package (R and Python strongly preferred)
    • Data visualization (Tableau strongly preferred)
  • Expertise in Excel modeling, with work or school experience in financial modeling a plus
  • Experience using data visualization tools, visual features of statistical software tools or PowerPoint to communicate complex ideas to non-technical audiences
  • Can share examples of working effectively in a team environment
  • Can share examples of how they have used data to solve complex problems
  • Can share examples of how they have used creativity to achieve excellent outcomes
