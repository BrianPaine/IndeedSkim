Business Intelligence Analyst (Richmond, VA)


 

 

Position Overview
 

At Impact Makers, we’re changing the way companies do business.  We are a social impact company and we don’t grow only for profit, we grow for purpose.  We scale not only for a greater bottom line, but also for greater impact.  Have you ever thought about going to work and making a difference for your client and the community?  Do you believe in social entrepreneurship?  If so you need to consider making a move to Impact Makers. 
 
Impact Makers is looking for a Business Intelligence Analyst consultant who aligns business strategies, goals, objectives and requirements with implementation of analytical products to join our growing Data Services practice.  Act as an analytics lead; leverage versatile technical capabilities to drive the development, evolution, and implementation of prototypes, models, processes, visualizations, dashboards, analytical models and solutions focused on driving fact based decision making.  Provide meaningful insights to clients through analytical products.
 

Skills & Qualifications

 
Responsibilities
  • Partner with clients to evaluate current and desired reporting and analytics capabilities through deep analysis of client’s data using various approaches, methodologies, and tools.
  • Collaborate in the development of Business Intelligence capabilities and products and ensure alignment of these capabilities to BI strategies and road maps.
  • Devise analytical models, dashboards, calculations, key performance indicators (KPIs), predictive models, insights and reports to meet business requirements and communicate insights to various levels of an organization. 
  • Drive improvements to data flow processes, data quality through analytical outputs.
  • Use industry leading tools such as Azure, AWS, Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft SQL, Cognos, SAS, Qlikview, Oracle etc. to develop technical solutions including data models, data warehouses, data marts, extract, transform and load processes (ETL), reports, visualizations and analytical outputs. 
  • Lead the process and data analysis for client engagements, gather requirements and document business needs, architectural components, data flow diagrams and other deliverables as needed.
  • Work with the team to design, develop and maintain data management and business intelligence solutions.
  • Develop business intelligence solutions and capabilities across the organization for analytics and data delivery.
  • Develop and consistently improve re-usable data repositories across multiple lines of business.
  • Build standards-driven, scalable and secure database queries, functions, stored procedures, extract, transform and load process (ETL), reports, visualizations and analytical outputs.
  • Collaborate with business users to gather requirements, develop action plans and implement solutions for data processes and reports in large enterprise solutions.
  • Work proactively with new and emerging technologies to assist with strategy and architecture for continuous improvement of data management and business intelligence functions.
  • Assist with implementation of business intelligence toolsets and act as a mentor; train business users and other staff.
  • Develop metadata for analytical products and source data.
  • Assist in the development of project estimates, plans and timetables.
 
Qualifications
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Business Analytics, Mathematics, Statistics or equivalent.  Master's degree preferred. 
  • Minimum 5 years of data management consulting experience, including the following:
  • Strong data manipulation skills (SQL or other) including 5+ years’ experience with an enterprise class database system such as SQL Server, Oracle, DB2 or Teradata.
  • Proven experience creating analytical products such as visualizations, dashboards, metrics, key performance indicators, cubes, analytical models, predictive models etc.
  • Proven experience and understanding of industry analytic packages and languages such as Tableau, SQL Server Reporting Services, Power BI, Cognos, Qlikview, SAS, R and Python.
  • Proven experience leveraging large, disparate datasets to provide enterprise level business insights and drive business outcomes.
  • Exposure and understanding of cloud data platform offerings such as Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services.
  • Strong desire to work with data in various forms (structured data warehouses/SQL, unstructured data environments/NoSQL/Big Data etc.).
  • Knowledge and experience working with secured and sensitive data and systems such as PII and HIPAA.
  • A broad, enterprise-wide view of the business needs and varying degrees of appreciation for data strategy, processes and capabilities and enabling data technologies.
  • Demonstrated analytical, problem solving, process improvement and documentation skills.  Familiar with structured analysis techniques including context diagrams, data flow diagrams, process modeling, functional decomposition models as well as conceptual, logical and physical data modeling.
  • Desire and commitment to continuous learning in data management, architecture, analytics and business intelligence.
  • Ability to meet strict deadlines in a high-volume environment.
  • Strong communications skills suitable for customer communications by phone, email, and in-person
  • Certifications a plus:  Certified Business Intelligence Professional (CBIP); Certified Data Management Professional (CDPM); Certified Data Vault Professional 2.0; Certified Enterprise Data Management Expert; Certified Data Steward; Information Governance Professional; and other certifications provided through various data-related products such as Oracle, Teradata, etc
 
Keyword Skills:  analytics, business intelligence, data visualization, key performance indicators, dashboards, reports, predictive analysis, prescriptive analysis, ETL development, data warehousing/data vaults, data architecture, data modeling, data quality, data security, Oracle, DB2, Teradata, SQL Server
