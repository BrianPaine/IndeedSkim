JOB DESCRIPTION

Piper Companies is looking for a Data Scientist who is creative, innovative and intellectually curious who is interested in joining a local Healthcare Analytics team for an exciting opportunity to work in one of the world's leading, large-scale data insights team.

Responsibilities of the Data Scientist:

  • You will play an important part in designing and delivering statistical / machine learning studies and predictive analytics solutions in a range of challenging analytical areas.   
  • This team works hand-in-hand with statisticians, epidemiologists and disease area experts across the wider global team, leveraging a vast variety of anonymous information from sources such as electronic health records.

 Qualifications of the Data Scientist:

  • Experience of statistical / machine learning projects in academia or commercial sector end to end with proven delivery capability including capturing requirements, designing analysis plans, interfacing with clients and report / manuscript writing.
  • Excellent knowledge of supervised machine learning methods.
  • Strong programming skills in Python and/or R. Experience in SPARK or Scala is highly beneficial.

Compensation of the Data Scientist:

  • Competitive salary + bonus potential
  • Stimulating, Innovative, Fun & friendly global work environment
  • Extremely interesting and challenging work; cutting-edge group, one of the only companies doing this work in healthcare
  • Great benefits including great office culture, healthcare  and competitive compensation package
