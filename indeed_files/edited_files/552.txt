Department Overview:



This position will be part of the Corporate Business Intelligence Team,

specifically reporting into the US Metrics Manager. The CBIT team is the

initial contact within Operations for inquiries related to key risk and

performance indicators. The team is responsible for delivering over 100

regional reports each day to hundreds of internal clients. Team members should

be able to bring both technical skills and people skills to be able to interact

across cultures with global colleagues.
