Position Description:  The Accounting Operations Sr. Analyst provides insightful analysis of business activity related to Supplier Financial Services (SFS) within PepsiCo North America Shared Services (NASS). Key responsibilities include SQL query and VBA macro writing, and consolidating, analyzing and communicating data related to spend, trade liabilities, cash flow and Days Payable Outstanding (DPO). The position is also responsible for addressing performance gaps and opportunities through automation and performing ad hoc analyses that facilitate problem-solving and influence sound decision-making by the Business Units served by NASS. Primary customers include NASS leadership and associates, divisional controllers and PepsiCo cross-functional teams.   Key Responsibilities:

Use in-depth knowledge and expertise with various querying and reporting tools and languages to mine, consolidate and analyze data for ad hoc requests and research assignments
Develop and refine queries and reports that address the unique and evolving needs of the Business Units
Identify and effect systems and process improvement opportunities through automation and participate in cross-functional collaboration to help develop and implement solutions
Research, analyze and explain cash flow impacts relative to Accounts Payable
Review, analyze and report results on certain liability accounts for the Business Units on a period basis
Prepare and analyze KPI's and interpret analytical findings
Provide insights and analysis as needed in support of special projects
Manage one direct report

 The successful candidate will possess excellent analytical skills, be capable of building collaborative relationships to work effectively with others, and drive for results in a dynamic environment.   Minimum Job Requirements:

Degree in related field of study (e.g., Finance, Mathematics, Information Systems, Engineering, etc) preferred
At least 5+ years of relevant business experience and practical exposure in some of the following roles: financial planning and analysis, data mining and root cause analysis, summary and interpretation of financial data
Highly advanced Excel skills and proficiency in PowerPoint
ERP system reporting experience, preferably in both Oracle and SAP; BW experience a plus
SQL query and VBA macro writing expertise
Demonstrated excellent analytical skills
Strong written and verbal communication skills
