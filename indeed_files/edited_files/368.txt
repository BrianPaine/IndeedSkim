Position Summary



Responsible for performing in-depth data analysis and developing ad-hoc queries and conversion requirements to meet business needs. Deploy, troubleshoot and resolve data issues, conducting data research and ensure data quality.

&bull;&nbsp;Perform analysis and document conversion requirements to translate data sets into standardized formats.

&bull;&nbsp;Perform data analysis functions for assigned business unit including design and development of critical reports.

&bull;&nbsp;Perform structured quality assurance testing including the development of test plans and scripts.

&bull;&nbsp;Proactively identify data issues that need to be fixed and perform detailed data verification and analysis.

&bull;&nbsp;Responsible for the escalation and resolution of data issues.

&bull;&nbsp;Make process and/or system improvement recommendations to assist the business unit in achieving its goals and objectives.

&bull;&nbsp;Work across business units/teams to collect, aggregate, match, consolidate, and confirm data for quality and accuracy.

&bull;&nbsp;Gather reporting requirements for change requests and ad-hoc reports as requested.

&bull;&nbsp;May assist with acquisition, extraction, transforming and standing up of various data sets.

&bull;&nbsp;May design and/or develop and ensure the quality of specific databases for collection, tracking and reporting of research data.

&bull;&nbsp;Performs other related duties as assigned.



&nbsp;



Requirements

&bull;&nbsp;Proficient written and oral communication skills in dealing with employees or external customers/clients

&bull;&nbsp;Experience working with data analytics-driven solutions with sensitive data sources
