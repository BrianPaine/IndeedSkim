&nbsp;If you&rsquo;re looking for a career that transforms, inspires, challenges, and rewards you, then come join us! Verisk Analytics is a global supplier of risk assessment services and decision analytics for customers in a variety of markets, including insurance, healthcare, financial services, supply chain, and others. We&rsquo;re a thriving public company with solid revenue growth and earnings and offices worldwide. &nbsp;And we&rsquo;re continually looking for ways to augment our existing markets and expand into new markets with excellent growth potential. At Verisk, you&rsquo;ll be part of an organization that&rsquo;s committed to serving the long-term interests of our stakeholders, including the communities where we operate.
Position Summary:
Are you passionate about playing with P&amp;C insurance dataset and does connecting the data and drilling down to the root cause of the data issues excite you? Are you looking for an opportunity where you can both practice your technical trade while adding to your leadership, consulting and delivery experience to turn a career corner? If you've got technical chops, consulting skills and are a passionate data architect, please read on...
&nbsp;Responsibilities:

Analyze policy, agency, claim and loss data set for P&amp;C insurance organization
Work closely with BI technology vendors for performing proof of concept (POC) of new technologies for recommending new tools and technologies for internal and external stakeholders
Document data sources on client project and perform gap analysis to identify inflow of bad data into data warehouse and develop recommendations and solutions
gather business requirement for data warehouse and derive the data requirement and data fields for data warehouse implementation and modification
Design rationale of data warehousing from scratch (Conceptual, logical and physical modeling) for Data Warehouse implementations for Insurance Policy &amp; Claim system
Prepare data mapping document, technical design document for ETL developer
Development of test cases, sql scripts for Unit testing, performance testing
Work closely with ETL team for implementing data transformation rules, data validations in to the data warehouse
Responsible for assessing data gaps for P&amp;C Insurance clients and develop industry best practices and solutions for business problems for internal and external stakeholders
Responsible for working with business users and leading all testing efforts for Unit, QA and User acceptance testing phase

&nbsp;
Requirements:

&nbsp;5+ years of experience data architecture and data analysis practice in Insurance industry
Hands on experience in developing data validation test cases for data warehouse development with star and snowflake schema
2+ years of hands on experience in Insurance Data model and/or BDW and able to create/maintain/coordinate business glossaries with project stakeholders
Data Mapping hands on expertise, able to understand business requirement and derive data dictionary and data assets requirement for ETL and DBA group
&nbsp; Expertise in any of the tools preferable any two of CA ERWIN, JIRA, Quality Center TestDirector
&nbsp; &nbsp;Understanding of ETL tools, able to interact with ETL development team and create source to target mapping and technical specifications for ETL developer
&nbsp;Able to create physical model and develop database architecture for DBA&rsquo;s to implement the model in database by developing rationale for storage allocation, partitioning strategy, archiving strategy
&nbsp; Strong SQL is a must and previous experience in any of the database Oracle, SQL Server, DB2
&nbsp; &nbsp;Knowledge and/or experience in the P&amp;C insurance industry is mandatory&ndash; along with at least one functional area relevant to these clients &ndash; new business, agency, risk management, actuarial, claims and loss
&nbsp; &nbsp;Thorough understanding of data warehouse architectures and data models for Insurance and financial services companies
&nbsp; &nbsp;Good team player
&nbsp; &nbsp;Strong project management skills and attention to detail
&nbsp; &nbsp; Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal

&nbsp;We are offering an excellent compensation package. Our competitive benefits package includes full health care options, a 401(k) plan, and generous Paid-Time-Off.
If this opportunity looks exciting and challenging to you, please click Submit Now to apply.
