		POSITION SUMMARY:Reporting to the Lead of Strategic Analytics, the Data Analyst participates on a team to meet the information, reporting, and analytic needs of Steward Health Care Network (SHCN).  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:  As an experienced member of the Analytics Team, the Data Analyst performs the following functions:•&nbsp;Develop comprehensive, timely and accurate analyses, reports and presentations on utilization, care retention, risk performance, care management, and quality metrics on population health efforts contracts for SHCN•&nbsp;Develop, compile and disseminate key reports and dashboards to SHCN leadership teams•&nbsp;Provide support to key analytic initiatives by consolidating disparate data files and cleaning data so analyses and reporting is accurate•&nbsp;Support ad hoc analytic requests, providing accurate and timely data, analysis and insightful interpretations•&nbsp;Support senior analysts and managers with analytic projects and development of performance/projection models•&nbsp;Work with SHCN staff&nbsp; (preferred, but not required)•&nbsp;Excellent verbal and written communication skills, outstanding interpersonal skills and ability to relate positively with individuals at all levels of the organization•&nbsp;Creative, flexible, and self-motivated with sound judgment;
