Our patients deserve the best in healthcare, and we deliver.Job Description
Provides technical support to assist in collection, analysis, and reporting of data to process improvement and regulatory compliance.
Essential Job Duties

Data Collection - Downloads, transfers, collects, and formats data from various Intermountain data sources. Coordinates and oversees data input or entry activities using knowledge of database structure and reporting capabilities.
Programming - Designs, develops, and tests queries and programs for collecting data using knowledge of spreadsheets and database software programs.
Measurement - Creates and maintains databases, using knowledge of database software, for collection and tracking of data as it relates to performance measurement.
Customers - Works with internal customers to develop and prepare quantitative reports in using data sources to analyze clinical and operational issues.

Posting Specifics

Benefits Eligible: No
Shift Details: Part-time
Department: Inservice Education
Location: Murray, UT

Minimum Requirements

Two years of data analysis experience.
Demonstrated knowledge of data collection, data quality control, report generation, and database file manipulation, or social and medical research methodology.
Demonstrated excellent spreadsheet skills, including: functions; filtering; and pivot tables.
Demonstrated proficiency in computer skills, including querying with inner and outer joins.
Demonstrated excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to work with multi disciplinary teams.
Demonstrated excellent organizational skills and an ability to identify analyze and solve problems.

Physical Requirements

Interact with others requiring the employee to communicate information.
Operate computers and other IT equipment requiring the ability to move fingers and hands.
See and read computer monitors and documents.
Remain sitting or standing for long periods of time to perform work on a computer, telephone, or other equipment.
