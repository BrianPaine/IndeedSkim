           								Junior Analyst

							

							

						

					

				

				

				

					

					

						

						   Department: Analytics

							   							   Office: CT

							   

						

					

					

				



				

				

					

						

							Responsible for providing data-driven insight and practical, proactive optimization recommendations across the Primacy client base. This individual will support the evolution of Primacy Analytics capabilities and work with the Performance Analytics Manager and Senior Strategist to provide the world-class analysis and campaign optimization necessary to accomplish their strategic objectives. By consistently delivering value, Primacy analytics team will become an inseparable part of our client relationships.						

					

				

				



				

				

					

						

							Responsibilities&nbsp;



Media Analytics: Monitoring and reporting of brand conversations, analysis of the quantity and sentiment of the conversations, measurement of Paid Search efforts showing clear tie between workstreams and ROI, and development of media segments/profiles

Website Funnel/Experience Reporting: Identification of friction points (errors, lack of engagement, abandonment, etc.) in online conversion processes and digital experiences

Measurement and Test Planning: Development of data infrastructure, test plans, learning objectives, measurement methodology, tool selection, implementation, measurement and ongoing process refinement

Behavioral Segmentation: Isolation of key segmentation variables, segment of users into actionable groups

Assist in the planning and implementation of Tagging defined in Analytics Architecture



Qualifications



1-2 years of Digital Marketing analytics experience

Ideally Google Analytics certified or willing to obtain certification within 90 days

Working knowledge of Google platforms including Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager

Proficient with MS Excel and PowerPoint

Familiarity with data visualization platforms such as Google Data Studio and DOMO and A/B testing platforms such as Google Optimize a plus

Eager to learn new technologies
