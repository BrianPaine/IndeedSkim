				Title

				Entry Level Data Scientist (June 2018)

			

			

				City

				Washington

			

			

				State

				DC

			

			

				Description

				Capgemini Government Solutions is searching for undergraduate and graduate level applicants who are motivated about growing their technical skills and desire to learn the fundamentals of Government consulting. You will work as part of a team helping to

 solve challenging issues by learning to use industry best-practices and a variety of technologies.





With more than 190,000 employees worldwide and multi billion dollars in revenue, Capgemini is a leading global provider of consulting, technology, and outsourcing services. Our team is one of the few global players that can offer US government agencies a full

 range of advisory, implementation, and management services to drive innovative ways to streamline organizations, reduce costs, and stay operationally effective. We are seeking innovative, strategic thinking, and entrepreneurial candidates interested in a Technical

 Analyst position. If you’re motivated and looking for an opportunity in the consulting profession, apply today!





Responsibilities may include: 

• Derive and visualize previously unknown insights from structured and unstructured data



• Engage in critical problem solving through data modeling, mining, predictive analytics, pattern recognition, machine learning, and statistics

• Assist in the design and implementation of data analytic applications by analyzing client business requirements to translate needs into technical requirements

• Use strong communications skills to interact with team members and clients on a daily basis

• Create timely and accurate documentation for all work products

• Implement tasks in accordance with schedule 

• Continuously improve skills, adapt emerging technologies, and apply best practices



Required:

• Must be a U.S. citizen and be eligible for a government security clearance

• Bachelor’s Degree completed prior to start in Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Applied Statistics, Machine Learning, Data Science, Operations Research, or Computer Science

• 0 to 1 years’ experience working as a data scientist 



Practical experience with one or more of the following:

• Development using Java, Python, R, or SAS

• Implementation and validation of predictive models and create and maintenance of statistical models with a focus on big data

• Analysis of data using regression, time series, mixed model, Bayesian, or clustering methods



Optional:

• Configuring and working with the Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP (LAMP) stack

• Knowledge of relational database technologies (e.g., Oracle, SQL Server, MongDB, MySQL, PostgreSQL)

• Experience with Agile inspired methodologies, coding guidelines, and best practices



Company Overview



Capgemini, one of the world's foremost providers of consulting, technology, and outsourcing services, enables its clients to transform and perform through technologies.





Capgemini Government Solutions, LLC (Capgemini GS) is a subsidiary of Capgemini focused on providing high quality Capgemini services to the U.S. Federal Government.





Capgemini GS provides equal employment opportunities (EEO) to all employees and applicants for employment. In addition to federal law requirements, Capgemini GS complies with applicable state and local laws governing nondiscrimination in employment.
