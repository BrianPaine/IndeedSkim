				Data Analytics Intern

									Slack is looking for interns to work alongside our data analysts for the summer of 2018. These internships are a unique opportunity for someone pursuing an MBA or PhD in a quantitative field who wishes to gain hands-on experience working with data to help improve product  and business team decisions in a startup setting. You will have the opportunity to work with a small and collaborative team and will be paired with one of our product data analysts, who will act as your mentor throughout the course of the program. Together you will help Slack’s product, sales, marketing or finance teams understand how people use Slack, set goals, prioritize projects, and evaluate the impact of their work. 
You should:

Be comfortable in a programming in a language of your choice (PHP, Python, R, Matlab, etc.) We mostly work in SQL but it is not a prerequisite for this internship


Understand the fundamental concepts of probability and statistics
Have strong quantitative and logical reasoning skills
Have excellent communication skills and be able to tell a clear story using data 
Be graduating December 2018 or later

You'll also have:

A positive attitude and empathy for our users
Self awareness and a desire to improve your skills
A curiosity about how things work
An eagerness to jump in and help fix things
