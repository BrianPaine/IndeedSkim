                 Requisition ID 104502BR          Date updated 10/04/2017     

        This is a temporary position for Raytheon for an undetermined length Job Description: The RMS business development team is a seeking a talented, investigative data analyst to support our growth oriented team. The ideal candidate will rigorously question assumptions, determine how to most convincingly display data and help lead change management and growth efforts. This position is responsible for performing data analytics to support Business Development and Product Line in making informed decisions with regards to forecasting and planning. The lead will help the respective BD team in defining key performance indicators (KPIs) and help establish action oriented methods of presenting the data. This will be a lead data analyst position, supplying valuable information such as win rates, assessment of growth plans, progress to goals, and more, to Business Development and RMS Business Leaders. In addition to providing data, this position will assist the Director, BD Ops with operational tasks including the administration of NBI and Enterprise Campaign dollars post allocation, updating progress to annual BD goals. This lead will work in collaboration with a council of data analyst to share methodologies and results in order to refine best practices across the organization. Major Responsibilities Include:Perform analysis using internal and external datasets – excels at seeing patterns between datasets to deliver actionable information. Seeks to understand business model and business environment – applies this knowledge towards proactively developing innovative data analysis, dashboards or other resources to help team achieve goalsChange agent – seeks opportunities for how decisions can be improved with data Creatively develops new formats for displaying data in actionable ways using resources such as Tableau Continuous learner – has a passion for learning about data management topics (big data, machine learning, etc.) Performs analysis using structured and unstructured data sources Works across cross-functional teams to deliver timely information to key stakeholders Provide monthly, quarterly and annual metrics to support RMS growth plans and provide the analytics to supports achievement of those plansProvide metrics and analysis that support New Business Investment (NBI) allocation and administrationWill develop a partnership and collaborate with members of the RMS SLT, RMS Program Council, RMS BDE’s, and other members of the RMS leadership team.Required Skills:Minimum of 2 years relevant experience specifically in data analysisAbility to obtain and maintain a DoD Security ClearanceSelf-starter able to work without direct supervision Experience with web-based CRM (Customer Relationship Management) applications or equivalent systems Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.). Must be proficient in pivot table and other Excel analysis methods Desired Skills: Intermediate understanding of SQL and database architecture principalsFamiliar with visualization tools (e.g. Tableau, Crystal Reports, etc.) Comfortable with public speaking and able to lead classroom training sessions A critical thinker and change leader with ability to solve complex, time-sensitive problems – a strategic thinkerExcellent written and verbal communication skillsSuperior ethical standards beyond reproachMinimum Education Required: Bachelor’s Degree in business, finance, information systems or related field, MBA preferred“This position requires a U.S. person or the ability to obtain an Export Authorization from the appropriate government agency for non-U.S. persons.” 104502
