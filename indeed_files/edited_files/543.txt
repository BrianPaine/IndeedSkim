The Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst will be responsible for developing reports, dashboards, scorecards, and other data products primarily using MicroStrategy (and Oracle). This person will



play a key role on a BI and analytics team responsible for the growth and development of our clients expanding portfolio or reporting requirements. Candidate must be capable of delivering high-quality deliverables, on time, with careful attention to detail.



&nbsp;



RESPONSIBILITIES:



- &nbsp;Translate businessrequirements into technical specifications.



- &nbsp;Create report mockups and templates from business requirements.



- &nbsp;Develop and maintain reports, dashboards, and scorecards using MicroStrategy.



- &nbsp;Understand and query disparate database sources, including relational structures, dimensional data models and &nbsp; cubes.202 embedded calculations, and reporting views to support BI product development.



- &nbsp;Ability to create and maintain ETL routines (between source systems and target BI environment)



- &nbsp;Test and configure BI reports and visualizations for development, testing and deployment.



- &nbsp;Work collaboratively in a small, cross-functional team.



- &nbsp;Interface with, and understand analytical reporting needs of business stakeholders.



- &nbsp;Ability to effectively communicate (written and verbal) technical subject matter to non-technical



business &nbsp;stakeholders.



&nbsp;



REQUIRED:



- &nbsp; Bachelor&#39;s Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or related discipline.



- &nbsp; Ability to write complex SQL queries.



- &nbsp; Experience with Oracle databases (or equivalent)



- &nbsp;US Citizen with ability to obtain required security clearance



&nbsp;



DESIRED:



- &nbsp; Minimum of 2 years of experience with at least one major BI tool such as MicroStrategy (strongly



preferred), SAP Business Objects, IBM Cognos, or OBIEE.



- &nbsp; Knowledge of ETL best practices and dimensional modeling.



- &nbsp; Experience documenting BI requirements and developing reports based on user requirements.&nbsp;



- &nbsp; Proficient with Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.



&nbsp;
