                    	Job Description

                      

	Coca-Cola Beverages Florida (&ldquo;CCBF&rdquo;), headquartered in Tampa, Florida, is one of the largest independently owned and operated Coca-Cola bottlers in the United States. CCBF markets, sells, and distributes products of The Coca-Cola Company (TCCC) in Florida.



	Coca-Cola Beverages Florida seeks to add a key member to its team by employing a dynamic Analyst.



	Analysts work with a variety of stakeholders to understand key business opportunities and deliver the analytical support needed to make value creating business decisions.&nbsp; Primary duties are typically separated into three categories: (i) definition of business opportunity (ii) solution formulation and analysis, and (iii) project management.



	Definition of Business Opportunity:



	

		Redefine business problems as solution hypotheses that can be answered through data mining and analytics

	

		Perform in-depth research to help identify new investment opportunities and develop strategic investment rationales

	

		Bring a fresh, objective voice to business discussions, and facilitate decisions grounded in data, not just previous experience





	Solution Formulation and Analysis:



	

		Evaluate prospective solutions for coherence with company strategy and priorities

	

		Demonstrate analytical skills, employing statistical modeling techniques, to create tools to answer business questions

	

		Build robust, flexible financial models that support traditional valuation methodologies and sensitivity analyses

	

		Consult and coordinate with a variety of subject matter experts, including operations, finance, legal, and third-party advisors to ensure solutions can be executed in an integrated way across the entire value chain

	

		Document core business processes and work with subject matter experts to facilitate the design of business process changes

	

		Communicate evaluation of business opportunities, analysis results, and action recommendations to relevant senior professionals via verbal and written reports and presentations, including preparing qualitative and quantitative reports, memoranda, and spreadsheets





	Project Management:



	

		Assist in realization of value capture opportunities by seeing solutions through to implementation

	

		Track and monitor project management activities and review progress with relevant stakeholders

	

		Provide hands-on support to business teams, as necessary





	Other Responsibilities:



	

		Help develop team members in modeling, project management and other aspects of the analytics process

	

		Perform related work and ad-hoc projects, as assigned





	&nbsp;

                    		Skills & Requirements

                      

	Education:



	

		Bachelor&rsquo;s degree from an accredited four-year college or university with major coursework in statistics, data analysis, economics, engineering, finance, accounting, operations research or related areas

	

		Master&rsquo;s degree may substitute for one (1) year of the required experience





	Experience:



	

		Two (2) years of full-time professional experience involving a high degree of skill in data analysis, modelling and evaluation, such as statistics, financial analysis, management consulting or engineering.





	Preferred Qualifications:



	

		Experience in investment banking, consulting and/or risk underwriting

	

		Experience with SAP Enterprise systems &amp; applications

	

		Broad knowledge of statistical modeling and data mining methodologies and tools
