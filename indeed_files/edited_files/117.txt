&nbsp;

At Northrim Bank, our vision is to be Alaskas premier bank and employer of choice! We are looking for professional and knowledgeable employees who take pride in their work. We believe in providing value for our customers and communities.

&nbsp;

Job Duties &amp; Functions:

The primary function of the Data Analyst position is to perform data analysis and create reports, extracts, and other sources of business intelligence that drive the Banks business operations, and as such will need to exercise great discretion and confidentiality.&nbsp;

 This position is expected to implement and enforce technology-related policies and procedures that affect the security and integrity of Bank data.&nbsp; This position will execute major business intelligence related projects that affect the Bank's business operations.&nbsp;

 This includes using advanced systems analysis techniques and procedures, as well as working directly with vendors and end-users to determine proper reporting specifications.&nbsp; This position will provide the first level of business intelligence support for Bank

 employees and therefore requires a high level of analytical skills to create and resolve complex reporting requests in addition to excellent communication skills.&nbsp; This position ensures all service and report requests are completed in a reasonable timeframe

 and to our customers satisfaction. This position reports directly to the Data Analytics Group Manager.

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

Job Qualifications:



Education: Associate's Degree from an accredited institution of higher learning required, preferably in the field of Computer Science or related field of expertise.&nbsp;



Education may be considered and substituted with two years commensurate experience.



Experience: Six (6) years of experience working in the information technology field to include:



Three (3) years of experience querying and analyzing data, and report authoring, preferably in the financial sector.

Related military education and experience may be used as a substitute for education and experience.





&nbsp;

In addition to the above requirement, this position requires:



Must be a motivated, a self-starter, have strong listening and problem-solving skills, and is able to handle challenging situations with limited supervision.

Must be able to multi-task and accomplish a high degree of accurate work that adheres to established processes, procedures and regulations.

Ability to independently analyze, prioritize and resolve issues. High levels of analytical, spatial, and mathematical abilities are required in order to determine solutions to varied, difficult and complex problems. Must remain thorough and detail oriented

 while performing several tasks concurrently. Must enjoy working in a fast-paced environment that requires attention to detail and accuracy.

Must have a basic understanding of reporting methodologies and how reports convey data to users.

Must have a strong knowledge and proven experience of query and reporting languages, such as SQL, PL/SQL, and T-SQL. Must have a strong understanding of business intelligence software, such as SAP Business Objects, PeopleSoft, Crystal Reports, and SSRS.

 Must have an intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Windows and be able to utilize and understand general computer concepts.&nbsp; Must be proficient with standard office machinery such as; PC (keyboard and ten-key), telephone, printer, and fax/copier.&nbsp; Must have

 strong knowledge of Microsoft Excel as used to retrieve and analyze data. Must be able to navigate through computerized and manual resources in order to identify and resolve problem situations. Must possess intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite.
