            Job Opening

            Job Title: Data Reporting Analyst

            

                Clark Associates, Inc specializes in providing foodservice equipment and supplies to restaurants, hospitality, and healthcare businesses around the world. Products are sold through three channels of distribution including TheWebstaurantstore.com (Ecommerce Model), The Restaurant Stores (Cash and Carry Model), and Clark Food Service Equipment (Traditional Distribution Model). The procurement department is responsible for managing the 180,000 items available to our three distribution channels.

We are currently seeking a Data Reporting Analyst to join our growing Procurement Department, based in Lancaster, PA.&nbsp; The Data Analyst will provide insight and decision support to management. This position reports directly to upper management.&nbsp; The successful candidate must have an interest in working with a large amount of data, facts and figures and seeking an opportunity to add value to the company by analyzing a variety of data sources that lead to strategic recommendations.&nbsp; This position will work primarily with data involving pricing, inventory and logistics.

Job Tasks and Responsibilities:



Lead and conduct analytics projects, providing detailed analysis, both written and verbal.





Ensure data is carefully gathered

Interpret data, determine trends and come to new conclusions based on the findings

Conduct research and integrate findings into databases and reporting

Present findings and translate the data

Conduct all activities, including producing final presentations, with a high attention to detail



Key Requirements/Skills/Experience



Bachelor&rsquo;s degree in Analytics, Statistics, Math, or other quantitative field

Expert or Advanced level proficiency with Microsoft Excel.&nbsp;

Knowledge on building databases in Microsoft Access

Self-starter with ability to manage projects independently

Ability to handle fast-paced professional office environment





Strong analytical and problem-solving skills





Highly organized and strong attention to detail



No industry knowledge needed we will train you.&nbsp; Compensation includes competitive wages, a health care plan, 401K plan and paid time off.&nbsp; Other job perks include standing desks, on site gym, commercial kitchen in break room and monthly theme lunches and social events. &nbsp;Please include a cover letter with your application. &nbsp;
