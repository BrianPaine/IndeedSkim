                    	Job Description

                      

 Strategy and Management Services, Inc. (SAMS) offers a variety of enterprise-wide, complex services and solutions to our customers. SAMS exceeds expectations with people who make a difference by providing support to our clients in the areas of Operations, IT, and Facilities Management. Our people make a difference to clients!



 Due to continued growth, we are currently seeking a Data Analyst at Springfield, VA. This position is contingent upon contract award.&nbsp;In this position, he/she will be responsible for supporting a project which provides all Case Management services necessary to enable the job placement of displaced defense contractors (including veterans, members or the National Guard/Reserves and transitioning service members) across the Commonwealth.



 Major areas of responsibility or tasks performed critical to success:



 &bull; Perform data analysis and conduct studies of existing data and techniques



 &bull; Have the ability to support and maintain case management for targeted individuals across the Commonwealth. Identify the number the individuals that could be served.



 &bull; Be able to help monitor and document important benchmarks for the industry.



 &bull; Be able to help develop and provide case management services designed for large groups of diverse industries and employers.



 &bull; Have demonstrated the ability to collect and analyze data from case management services and help provide timely detailed reports.

                    

                    

                    

                    		Skills & Requirements

                      

 Qualification:



 &bull; Bachelor&rsquo;s Degree from accredited university desired



 &bull; Five or more years of experience required



 &bull; Must be able to collect and analyze data and provide regular reports of all program parameters.



 &bull; Must have exceptional communication and collaboration skills, and the ability to solve problems.
