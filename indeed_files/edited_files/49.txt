   Housekeeper

The right candidate understands the need for a high-level of guest service as they would be a part of a team to make the guest’s experience excellent and memorable.  Proven reliability is needed.  Part and full-time positions are available; mornings, weekends and holidays are required and a part of the normal schedule.  

 To apply please contact Becca at 413-400-3333 or email your resume to  innkeeper@thirtythreemain.com. 

   Assistant Innkeeper   

 Assistant Innkeeper is needed for a high-end, 8 room Inn located in the heart of Lenox.  Previous experience working in an Inn or Bed & Breakfast is highly desired.  The right candidate would be an organized person who multi-tasks with ease whose highest motivation is to provide the guests with an excellent and memorable experience that is beyond their expectations.  Duties will include guest relations including checking in and out, making reservations, communicating with guests via telephone and email, coordinating with housekeeping and maintenance for swift resolutions to any problems.  Making and serving breakfast to guests will also be a responsibility.  The ideal person is familiar with the local area including most cultural attractions.   A positive & friendly attitude is required, excellent verbal and written skills are needed.  Must be available for flexible hours including mornings and evenings, the regular schedule will include weekends and holidays. 

 To apply please contact Becca at 413-400-3333 or email your resume to  innkeeper@thirtythreemain.com.

     Data Analyst    


  Primary Responsibility:   
 	Collect, organize, and analyze product lifecycle data, in order to make data-driven recommendations to drive revenue and profitability.  

 	Analyze business procedures and recommend specific requirements that will allow product data to be more accurate and accessible.

   Day-to-Day Responsibilities:   
 Gather, format, and analyze data throughout the product lifecycle.

  Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns using statistical techniques.

  Format reports in charts, graphs and tables that easily translate into actionable items.

  Monitor product performance, identify improvements, and provide ongoing reports.
li>

  Conduct full lifecycle analysis to include data requirements, structure, and design.

 Maintain the highest level of accuracy of product data by filtering and cleaning data.

  Provide quality assurance of imported data by monitoring master data including creation and updates.


    Evaluated On:  
 
  Accuracy and reliability of data reports. 

  Consistency of product data. 

  Quality, validity, and credibility of analysis. 

 Ability to produce actionable recommendations based on data.
 
 

    Requirements:  
   Accuracy and attention to detail are paramount.

  Ability to number crunch large amounts of data using Excel pivot tables and other database applications.

  Understanding of basic statistical methods.

 


To apply please email your cover letter and resume to jmoro@annieselke.com.

First shift runs Mon-Friday, 7am-3:30pm
