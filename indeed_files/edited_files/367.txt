                                     Deloitte Services LP includes internal support areas such as Marketing and Communications, Human Resources/Talent, Information Technology, Facilities Management, and Financial Support Services. Application InsightsData Insights AnalystLocation: Hermitage/Nashville, TN About the teamThe Application Insights team serve our customers—the business and technical teams—with data-driven insights to guide data-focused, informed business and investment decisions and solve business challenges. The team prioritizes accelerating user adoption and measuring the success of applications. Their competencies range from establishing and maintaining client relationships, understanding client desired outcomes, setting performance metrics sets, defining KPI’s, establishing analytics tracks, to preparing data for analytic purposes, performing statistical analysis, creating data visualizations, and being able to develop insights and present them to different audiences through diverse mediums. Role DescriptionThe Data Insights Analyst will gather client requirements, collect, combine, visualize and analyze data from disparate sources to deliver relevant and actionable statistics and insights for business decision support.  The success of this candidate is predicated on providing insights that enable owners and stakeholders to:Gauge productivity and efficiency gains on developed applications and product features (where to invest further, or reduce investment)Leverage data assets and create outcome focused insights for decision supportProvide Change Management teams with insight on where to focus their efforts to improve efficiencies Job responsibilities:Access, clean, store, visualize, analyze, and interpret raw data culled from various data sources available for final consumption by clients of differing levels of technical/business understanding and need, as appropriate.Create relevant and visually compelling data visualizations that incorporate advanced filtering and functionality for optimal data interaction for analysts and business users.Identify key application features, define and deliver KPIs for business applications.Analyze customer usage data, identify patterns and derive insights for application and client or portfolio success.Correlate different data points and attributes to identify clusters of opportunity for business process improvement, practitioner productivity gains, cost savings or analytic insight.Transform data points and audience measurement into compelling insights in clearly distilled and displayed deliverables that effectively communicate the relevant and reliable messages. Independently manage project deadlines to ensure the timely delivery of requested insights.Contribute ideas and assist in cultivating concepts during team brainstorm gatherings, working on special projects and partnering with peers and other data departments.
