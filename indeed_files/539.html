Data Analyst - Intern

Job description

Intern will assist the analytics team in developing reports as well as troubleshoot data issues. Will have a very fine eye for detail, and deep understanding of the popular data analysis tools and databases.

Requirements

Knowledge/Skills Requirements:

  • Currently studying graduate or senior undergraduate in mathematics, statistics, computer science or related field. 
  • Someone comfortable forming new relationships quickly in order to work with different departments/people within the organization early on.
  • Able to lead without authority
  • Excellent computer skills specifically with MS PowerPoint, MS Excel, MS Word, (MS Visio preferred as well but not required)
  • Good project management skills
  • Prefer someone who is interested and knowledgeable with UI/UX design
