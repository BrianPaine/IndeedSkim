Facilitates and promotes patient management, continuity of care, and cost effectiveness through the integration of case management, utilization review, and discharge planning; facilitates the collection and provision of quality data for the physician Peer Review Committee.

Regulatory Requirements:

  • Associate's degree required, bachelor degree preferred.
  • Current Registered Nurse Licensure, within state of Colorado.
  • Minimum of 1 year experience with clinical operations and systems development, or analytics/statistics, or the equivalent combination of education and experience preferred.
  • Previous experience with electronic medical records preferred.

Language Skills:

  • Able to communicate effectively in English, both verbally and in writing.
  • Additional languages preferred.
  • Excellent customer service skills

Skills:

  • Basic computer knowledge.
  • Requires excellent communication, analytical, facilitation and negotiation skills, Data Process Modeling, knowledge of competition, industry happenings and knowledge of how the Company is positioning itself.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Analytical and facilitation skills required.
  • Experience in any of the following is a plus:
    • Current health care regulations and standards
    • Current industry issues
    • Knowledge of the company
