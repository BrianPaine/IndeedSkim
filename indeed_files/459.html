Data Analyst
Summary
Title:Data Analyst
ID:1087
Department:Sales
Salary Range:$11/hr
Description
Grow Op Farms, the leading I-502 producer by volume of top-shelf cannabis in the state of Washington is hiring a Data Analyst for their Spokane location. This position is responsible for the evaluation of sales data, and with that data helping to identify trends, patterns, and areas of opportunity for our sales team. This position is expected to support and adhere to Grow Op Code of Ethics and Business Standards.

Responsibilities
  • Evaluates client account information (purchases, past order history).
  • Analyzes market data.
  • Identifies current inventory.
  • Recommends future orders and sales.
  • Partners with clients on their sales orders.
  • Performs all other duties/responsibilities as necessary or assigned.
Qualifications
  • to perform this job successfully, an individual must have:
  • a High School Diploma or a General Education Degree or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Advanced knowledge of spreadsheets, including proficiency in Microsoft Offices products (i.e. Word and Excel) and Google Drive (i.e. Docs and Sheets).
  • Previous experience with data analysis preferred.
