Job summary

The Red Hat Customer Experience and Engagement (CEE) Analytics team is looking for a Website Data Analyst to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you will be responsible for analyzing Red Hat Customer Portal traffic patterns and user behavior to improve site usability and content relevance. You'll also assist with strategic initiatives and providing actionable, data-driven insights to stakeholders. As a Website Data Analyst, you'll partner with business leads, product managers, content development teams, and other stakeholders to understand and achieve business priorities through data analysis and reporting and translate complex findings into meaningful stories and visualizations.

Primary job responsibilities

  • Analyze web analytics data and develop reports and dashboards that help optimize outcomes and shape business decisions
  • Help guide tagging and tracking implementation, maintenance, and improvement initiatives for the Customer Portal
  • Consult with relevant teams on A/B testing and other web optimization initiatives
  • Advise on interpretation of web analytics data and provide insights and recommendations to the teams that support the Customer Portal
  • Monitor multiple data streams, including search and user data, to report on specific trends and assist with Customer Portal optimization decisions
  • Assist with defining and tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) for the Customer Portal
  • Perform ad hoc analysis and provide analytical consultation and support to key stakeholders

Required skills

  • Proficiency in collecting and analyzing data from Adobe Analytics or Google Analytics web reporting tools
  • Experience with A/B testing, multivariate testing, or website optimization tools like Adobe Target or Google Optimize
  • Advanced data visualization skills with experience reporting to executive level; experience with tools like Qlik (Sense, View) or Tableau is a plus
  • Experience managing Adobe Analytics or Google Analytics web tracking implementation
  • Experience with tag management tools like Adobe DTM or Google Tag Manager
  • Solid organizational, problem-solving, and analytical skills
