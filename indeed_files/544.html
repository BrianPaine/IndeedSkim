Data Scientist

Boston, Massachusetts, United States · Technology Development

Description

DRG is focusing on adding large scale “real-world data” into its business. This is a combination of medical insurance claims data, prescriptions data or information from medical record system covering a large portion of the relevant patient population. In order to effectively manage the billions of records that make up this type of data, DRG is building out real world data (RWD) infrastructure based on a distributed computing platform.

The Data Scientist will be focused on analyzing data and developing suitable algorithmic methods for connecting data sets or otherwise refine the information contained in our real-world data assets.

ROLE DESCRIPTION

  • Perform deep dives into our real-world data assets searching for patterns and insights in specific areas of internal or customer interest
  • Develop solutions to real-world data related problems using statistical or algorithm based approaches
  • Be part of the team implementing the RWD platform and its capabilities using an iterative approach
  • Work with Product Owner, Business Analysts, Architects and other stakeholders to participate in business requirements and produce technical designs and development plans.
  • Communicate complex technical topics to non-technical resources and leadership. Provide technical input on alternative approaches to issues that arise.
  • Communicate and enforce coding standards. Perform code reviews and support an Agile, Test Driven development methodology.
  • 5-10% travel to subsidiary sites and other locations

Requirements

  • Master's degree in computer science, statistics or related discipline
  • 3+ years of experience in a data analytics / science focused role
  • Expert knowledge in statistics, advanced methods, machine learning and related fields
  • Previous experience with distributed computing platforms such as Spark or Hadoop is a plus.
  • Background in using open source technology and databases
  • Experience with Agile development methodology
  • Prior experience working in the healthcare/pharmaceutical or data-driven B-to-B applications is a plus

COMPANY INFORMATION

Decision Resources Group (DRG) is a global information and technology services company that provides proprietary data and solutions to the healthcare industry. With over 1,000 employees and 17 offices worldwide, we have brought together best-in-class companies to provide end-to-end solutions to complex challenges in healthcare. Our team of highly knowledgeable experts reframe these challenges, enabling our customers to see the opportunities. To learn more, please visit our website at https://decisionresourcesgroup.com.

Benefits

Decision Resources Group offers a competitive compensation package and medical, dental, vision and life insurance programs from first day of employment. We also offer a 401(K) retirement plan after two complete months of employment. We are an equal opportunity employer.
