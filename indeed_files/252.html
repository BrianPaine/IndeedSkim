Data Analyst

Join a team recognized for leadership, innovation and diversity

Join a company that is transforming from a traditional industrial company to a contemporary digital industrial business, harnessing the power of cloud, big data, analytics, Internet of Things, and design thinking. You will work with customers to identify their high value business questions and work through their data to search for answers. You will be responsible for working within Honeywell to identify opportunities for new growth and efficiency based on data analysis.

Honeywell is looking for a highly-qualified Data Analyst who will deliver superior analytic capabilities in the support of customer needs. The ideal candidate will provide a balance of data research and analysis, as well as being able to communicate clearly with internal and external customers. The analyst will need to be a self-starter who can work independently, navigating diverse data sources to find the answers to impossible questions.

Be a member of a team that delivers contemporary analytics solutions for all Honeywell business groups and functions. Join a high-performing team and distinguished talent pipeline.

25 Customer support
25 Advise senior management
20 Develop internal processes
10 Think creatively
20 Provide strategic advice

YOU MUST HAVE

  • Bachelors degree in Business, Computer Science, Journalism, History, Engineering, or related field
  • U.S. Citizenship required to obtain and maintain a government security clearance

WE VALUE

  • Some analytical work experience
  • Some experience with on-site customer experience, to include targeting, requirements management or data mining
  • Advanced skills using Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, writing formulas, V look-ups)
  • Experience with Windows networking, cyber systems and technologies with focus in areas such as Matlab, Perl, Python, SQL, Siebel, Tableau, and Analyst Notebook
  • Candidate should understand the basics of program management, but PMP certification is not required
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills for internal and external customers
  • Demonstrated knowledge of business objectives, processes and priorities

Exempt How Honeywell is Connecting the World

INCLUDES

  • Some Travel Required
