Job Description

Strategy and Management Services, Inc. (SAMS) offers a variety of enterprise-wide, complex services and solutions to our customers. SAMS exceeds expectations with people who make a difference by providing support to our clients in the areas of Operations, IT, and Facilities Management. Our people make a difference to clients!

Due to continued growth, we are currently seeking a Data Analyst at Springfield, VA. This position is contingent upon contract award. In this position, he/she will be responsible for supporting a project which provides all Case Management services necessary to enable the job placement of displaced defense contractors (including veterans, members or the National Guard/Reserves and transitioning service members) across the Commonwealth.

Major areas of responsibility or tasks performed critical to success:

• Perform data analysis and conduct studies of existing data and techniques

• Have the ability to support and maintain case management for targeted individuals across the Commonwealth. Identify the number the individuals that could be served.

• Be able to help monitor and document important benchmarks for the industry.

• Be able to help develop and provide case management services designed for large groups of diverse industries and employers.

• Have demonstrated the ability to collect and analyze data from case management services and help provide timely detailed reports.

Skills & Requirements

Qualification:

• Bachelor’s Degree from accredited university desired

• Five or more years of experience required

• Must be able to collect and analyze data and provide regular reports of all program parameters.

• Must have exceptional communication and collaboration skills, and the ability to solve problems.
