Data Analyst

DXC Technology (NYSE: DXC) is the world’s leading independent, end-to-end IT services company, helping clients harness the power of innovation to thrive on change. Created by the merger of CSC and the Enterprise Services business of Hewlett Packard Enterprise, DXC Technology serves nearly 6,000 private and public sector clients across 70 countries.

The company’s technology independence, global talent and extensive partner alliance combine to deliver powerful next-generation IT services and solutions. DXC Technology is recognized among the best corporate citizens globally. For more information, visit www.dxc.technology. 

DXC Technology has an immediate need in our US Public Sector group for a Seat Deployment Analyst- Secret Clearance in Boise, ID, Plano, TX, El Paso, TX, Dublin, OH, Charleston, WV, or Clarksville, VA.

Job Description:

• Regional Data Analyst is responsible for managing data for the USMC WIN 10 EUD Upgrade Project
• Monitor, Track, and Report HDD Inventory and DD250 forms for SIPR Hard drives
• Manually compare DD250 and HDD Inventory forms against deployment schedule ensuring that proper documentation is gathered and reported
• Analyze and interpret customer data submission orders and report discrepancies
• Aligning seats on WSM/BO with projects on tactical plan ensuring that all customer data is submitted accurately and on time in accordance to project timelines.
• Monitor , validate, requests for upgrade against current schedule
• Use Milestone tracker in SharePoint to input to track project milestones and report deficiencies
• Monitor orders and assist PMs with initial schedule creation
• Utilizing customer provided data pull into project for Site Data Schedule for project creation and assignment
• Create multiple reports and Metrics to include but no limited to Tactical Plan Report, Actuals Report, Enterprise Delivery Report, etc. , DXC Seat Delivery

Qualifications:
MS Project
MS Excel
Detailed ability to analyze and report on complex and dynamic projects
Able to quickly analyze manual schedules.
Analyze detailed spreadsheets.
Ability to obtain DoD security clearance
