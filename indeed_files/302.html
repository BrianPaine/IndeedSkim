Position Description:

Position Duties:

  • Ensure outstanding work-products are delivered to the various managers across the region.
  • Maintain various internal databases to optimize team performance.
  • Work adroitly with Access, Excel, and a variety of other proprietary programs.
  • Support management team by distributing weekly, monthly, and quarterly production reports.
  • Provide excellent customer service and troubleshooting by resolving technical hardware and software related issues.
  • Manage requests for new equipment and terminations.
  • Maintain inventory system and overseeing equipment returns and equipment fulfillment.
  • Assist with team projects, including building marketing materials, assisting with Group Reviews, and gathering promotional items to be shipped out.

 

 


Position Requirements:

Job Requirements

  • Strong Excel and Access Skills
  • Have the following education + experience levels:
  • Associates Degree (preferably in a business field) with 4 years’ work experience
  • Bachelor’s Degree with 1-2 years’ work experience
  • Possess strong organizational skills, reporting and analytic skills
  • Demonstrated aptitude in learning quickly, problem solving, communication, creativity, and teamwork
  • Have strong technology proficiency
  • Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Photoshop
  • Experience in web design with knowledge of HTML and CSS
