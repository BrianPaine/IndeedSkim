Junior Analyst

Posted Date:9/28/2017 12:30:39 AM
Job Type: Full Time
Req #: JR-08571
Location: Beverly Hills, CA
Job Category: Product Management

Job Summary:

Role:

Live Nation Digital Marketing is seeking a Junior Analyst that can help drive, pull, report, digest, and translate data. The role involves assessing high-impact marketing initiatives that contribute to company objectives. This individual must be highly analytical, can view the business from a number of different levels, can interact effectively with all levels of management, and work with stakeholders to identify and implement new opportunities to grow the business. The individual will distill data and key metrics into insights that will help shape the future of the direction of Live Nation marketing. 

Responsibilities:

  • Lead reporting, insights, analysis for all Live Nation web and app properties
  • Test/QA/implement tracking on Livenation.com and the Live Nation App
  • Ad Hoc/recurring reporting to support all marketing initiatives
  • Analyzing large volumes of data from disparate databases into distilled, actionable information needed by senior leadership to answer critical questions
  • Create well-organized presentations and reports with direction from senior project leads.
  • Determine success of new business partners through quantitative analysis

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree required
  • 1-2 years of work experience with web analytics and online marketing
  • Highly skilled in Excel; familiarity with SQL
  • Google Analytics and Omniture experience a must
  • Experience with Social Analytics (Facebook, Twitter, Simply Measured) or Salesforce a plus
  • Ability to automate using BI tools such as Tableau or DOMO
  • Relentless problem-solving abilities and desire for continued improvement
  • Comfort with large data sets and modeling
  • Strong PowerPoint skills
  • Outstanding communication, attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Assertive yet professional demeanor, persistence and willing to lead the way
  • Ability to interpret data for different personalities and areas of the business
  • Extraordinary team player and ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment where quality and execution are critical to day to day success
