Title:Data Reporting Analyst

Description
  • Write easy to understand instructions for running reports
  • Write easy to read report explanation documents
  • Assist with the organization of report development
  • Assist with and/or run daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly reporting per client requirements
  • Assists in the creation, evaluation and testing of existing or new reports
  • Create basic database queries to extract data for reporting
  • Run weekly and monthly, automated and/or manual, reports for internal departments and clients of a more complex nature
  • Run one off reports for internal and external departments as needed
  • Gather requirements from end users and/or Clients as they pertain to new reports or changes to existing reports.
  • Provide analytical insight into generated reports and data as it pertains to the business of ABC-Amega.

Required Education/Experience

Bachelor's Degree in MIS, Math, Statistics, or Business and 0-3 years' Experience.

Must have experience in MS Excel, MS Access, and familiarity with VBA and basic SQL.
