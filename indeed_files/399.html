Jr. Data Analyst

Location: New London, CT

Department: Buyers Edge

Type: Full Time

Min. Experience: Entry Level

Overview

Buyers Edge is a rapidly growing technology company in the foodservice purchasing space.  Our clients range from independent restaurants to large casinos and hotels such as Mohegan Sun, Caesars, MGM and Waterford Hotel Group. We perform unique analysis of pricing and purchase data and are looking for an organized, detail oriented individual to become an essential part of our team. The right person will welcome challenges, thrive in a small team environment, and be eager to learn new skills.

Summary

The Product Matcher will be responsible for mapping raw data to existing items in our database.  The ideal candidate will be highly productive while also maintaining complete accuracy.  Additionally, the best candidate would be adept at searching the internet, searching catalogs, and maximizing all sources of information in order to draw proper conclusions. On a daily basis this employee must be capable of utilizing logic and critical thinking in order to solve challenges that arise.  This position has a fixed workload each day and time management skills are essential.  Additionally the candidate must take pride in the quality of their work.  Over a period of time we would task this employee with initiatives to improve the quality of our data and help oversee that our standards are met.

Other Duties

  • Continually improve data quality
  • Reach out to business partners to acquire additional resources
  • Utilize our database to research the history of items
  • Release pending files to be processed in a methodical and organized way
  • Update colleagues and business partners with the status updates

Qualifications

Required Skills

  • High proficiency and comfortably with Microsoft Excel
  • Efficient, detail oriented, and well organized
  • Ability to maintain a high level of confidentiality
  • Comfortable with technology
  • Ability to multitask and meet deadlines
  • Ability to work autonomously as well as collaboratively, ready to produce results

Personal Skills

  • High attention to detail
  • Resourcefulness
  • Strong critical thinking skills
