Sales & Marketing: Database Marketing/Analytics
Data Analyst - Minnesota Wild (St. Paul, MN)

The Minnesota Wild Marketing Intelligence Group is a progressive and innovative team that uses data driven insights to provide information for leaders of the organization to remain in line with the Wild’s mission statement of creating a greater State of Hockey.

We are expanding our analytical team in search of an individual who can help lead system integrations and data management for our stakeholders across the organization. We are located in downtown St. Paul and work closely in a fun and relaxed environment that thrives with creative and passionate individuals.

This is exciting opportunity if you are…

  • ready to challenge yourself and be a part of a supportive, data driven environment
  • eager to share your SQL experience
  • want to be the go-to CRM guru for our stakeholders (we will train!)
  • have the ability to self-teach software skills and the ability to implement software processes
  • comfortable not knowing the answer
  • can translate technical data to non-technical users

What you’ll do:

In this new position, you will contribute directly to the strategic development and execution of CRM campaign management, oversee the data strategy and the mapping of data integrations. Your goal will be to ensure the reliability, integrity, and quality of customer data (ticket sales, merchandise, customer service, etc.) for the Minnesota Wild. You will manage the existing data environment, which includes guiding data strategy, expanding the data warehouse to include new data sources, and managing vendor relationships. You will thrive deep-diving into data quality issues that arise and will work to consistently provide quality data from multiple systems to various stakeholders.

What you’ll bring:

  • Bachelor’s degree & working knowledge in Microsoft SQL Server and relational databases
  • Experience working directly with a data warehouse
  • Experience creating and maintaining specific reporting using Tableau or reporting software, a plus
  • While knowledge of CRM campaign management is important, we have the tools and resources to develop our newest team member, if necessary.

What’s in it for you?

In addition to joining a fun and team-centered company that is invested into data analytics, you’ll enjoy the many great company benefits such as,

  • Comprehensive health benefits to fit your needs
  • 401(k) matching
  • On the job training
  • Free parking
  • Company sponsored events, activities and off-sites
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • On-site fitness center

As an officially designated Yellow Ribbon Company, we are committed to serving those who have served our country, we encourage service members and veterans to apply.
