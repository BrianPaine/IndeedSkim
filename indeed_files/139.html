Job Details


Requisition Number 17-1152
Post Date 9/29/2017
Title Data Analyst
Job Family Product Development
Location CA-Costa Mesa
Description Summary
• Responsible for the daily processing of incoming resident data files for specific Clients that have been assigned, based on the Clients’ incoming schedules.
• Review and resolve any exceptions and work items that have generated during the processing of the resident data files prior to billing.
• Monitor, track and distribute Outgoing A/R (Accounts Receivable) files based on Client specific schedules. Including the distribution of Move Out A/R files based on processing schedule.
• Monthly processing of rent data for participating Resident Onebill Clients.

Required Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:
• High school diploma or GED
• Strong verbal and written communication skills
• Strong computer skills – MS Office Excel (Intermediate-Advanced), Access, Word, Outlook
• Data mining and analytical skills
• Detail-oriented
• Ability to manage multiple projects/assignments at one time
• Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment
