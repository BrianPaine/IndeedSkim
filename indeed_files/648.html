Business Analyst

at RoundPoint Mortgage Servicing Corporation (View all jobs)
Charlotte

 

Position Overview

The Business Analyst with be an integral part in establishing, documenting, delivering, and maintaining  strategic business requirements in order to improve the operational efficiency of the company.  The Business Analyst must possess a Bachelor’s degree and be willing to work in a fast-paced environment.     The ideal candidate for this position possesses attention to detail, a go-getter attitude, functions well under pressure, has great written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to manage competing projects simultaneously.

Working Relationships

The Business Analyst reports to the Business Analytics Supervisor and must develop strong working relationships with business units, key stakeholders, and co-workers.

Key Tasks and Responsibilities

  • Understand the daily, monthly, and routine operations of various business units in order to identify areas of potential improvement and increased efficiency.
  • Work with business units to establish, develop, and document business requirements for Data and Reporting Services.
  • Work with Data and Reporting Services to integrate the business requirements from business units into the daily, monthly, and routine operations of the company.
  • Identify and articulate inconsistencies between stated business requirements and the technical delivery of business requirements.
  • Routinely report on the status of operations within each business unit. Operations may include Key Performance Indicators, Business Analytics Projects, and Technical Delivery Management.
  • Incumbent will be responsible for meeting RoundPoint’s commitment to compliance and operational excellence
  • Other duties as necessary or assigned to support the overall growth of RoundPoint

 

Minimum Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects
  • Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint)
