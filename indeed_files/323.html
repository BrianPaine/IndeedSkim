Job summary

The Red Hat PNT Planning and Analytics team is looking for a Data Scientist Intern to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you'll contribute to our understanding and modeling of the customer experience and life cycle. As a subscription-based company, Red Hat must identify, at scale, key issues that lead to customer satisfaction or dissatisfaction and create models which guide areas of investments in our solutions portfolio. 

Primary job responsibilities

  • Create models of types of customer interactions with solutions and resultant behavioral outcomes
  • Analyze high-volume, unstructured data sources to build new structured models for customer interactions and product consumption
  • Create visualizations of data for internal and external consumption
  • Collaborate with other developers and analysts across departments and technologies

Required skills

  • Experience creating predictive models to help businesses identify and understand key customer behaviors
  • Experience using open source statistical computing toolkits like R, Apache Mahout, and scikit-learn
  • Data manipulation experience using R and Python
  • Experience with analytical tools like Business Objects and SAS
  • Experience communicating key insights and findings to business stakeholders
  • Experience in statistics, economics, or other relevant applied mathematics discipline
  • Currently pursuing a bachelor’s or master’s degree in data science, mathematics, computer science, or related quantitative field
