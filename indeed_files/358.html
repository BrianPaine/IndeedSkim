Reporting Analyst

Charleston, South Carolina, United States Full-time

TITLE:                       Reporting Analyst

LOCATION:              Charleston, SC

STATUS:                  Full-time, exempt

Education:               Bachelor’s Degree preferred

 

SUMMARY:

Levelwing is seeking a highly-motivated individual with an analytical mindset to aid in the reporting of web traffic and performance. This individual must retain a high degree of curiosity and a passion for data analysis. The Reporting Analyst must be proficient in data aggregation and synthesis to power actionable findings and recommendations. This individual needs a positive “can do” mindset to meet a variety of business objectives.

 

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Monitors and reports on a multitude of datasets including but not limited to: Website Performance, Marketing Channel Effectiveness, User Behavior and Engagement, Primary and Secondary Conversion Metrics
  • Comprehensive understanding of Web Analytics tools and knowledge of platforms that integrate with and impact digital data collection
  • Construct reports on a weekly, monthly, and ad-hoc basis
  • Identify and troubleshoot data collection issues and anomalies
  • Educate and support teammates through recommended reporting efficiencies
  • Present information in a consumable and impactful manner
  • Collaborate with Implementation Specialists, Media Analysts, and Web Developers on data collection methods
  • Create processes that streamline data collection and reporting needs
  • Ensure high level of accuracy and attention to detail in all work
  • Create and maintain data sourcing references and Analytics Design blueprints
  • Think creatively to analyze metrics from a variety of perspectives with the goal of providing valuable insight

 

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS & ABILITIES:

  • Precise communications skills
  • Ability to learn new technology platforms in a timely manner
  • Complex dataset consumption
  • Comprehension of digital marketing channels and strategies
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Exceptional analytical skills
  • Ability to multi-task and simultaneously work in multiple work streams
  • Proficient on Microsoft Office Products
  • Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Google Data Studio, Power BI experience a plus

 

DISCLAIMER: The above job description is not an exhaustive list of all duties and responsibilities of the position and in no way is to be construed as a contract.  As with all positions, employment is “at will” or voluntary on both the part of the organization and the team member.  Employment is not for a set period of time and may be discontinued by either party for any reason with or without notice.
