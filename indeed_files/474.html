Job Description


CVE is looking for a passionate Data Reporting Analyst to turn data into information, information into insight and insight into business decisions.


This position will conduct full lifecycle activities to include requirements analysis and design, develop analysis and reporting capabilities, and continuously monitor data reports.


Duties and Responsibilities


Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports

Assist team with dashboard and report generation

Create and manage reports in Excel format

Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems

Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets

Filter in “clean” data, and review computer reports, printouts

Work closely with management to prioritize business and information needs

Qualifications


Proven working experience as a data analyst

Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages

Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing large datasets (Excel)

1-4 years of reporting analysis

Must be professional in conduct and practice, and also reliable

Creative, Responsive and adaptive in a dynamic environment

Familiarity with accounting, finance, and business development practices and principles

Ability to independently, resourcefully, and creatively research and implement new reporting solutions

Working knowledge of analysis and reporting tools

Daily


* Collect raw data and generate failure rate for QV1-3, Assy, and RF on a daily basis.

Weekly


* Collect raw data and generate failure rate for QV1-3, Assy, and RF on a weekly basis.

Collect and prepare/generate data for weekly presentations for QA manager meetings.

Collect UAR/Valid data from customers and generate data charts/report to prepare presentation for weekly Director Meetings.

Fill out, deliver, and monitor SCAR activity and close opened accounts per supplier.

Monitor QV productivity/cell logs provided by quality/best buy associate.

Monthly


Collect raw data and generate failure rate for QV1-3, Assy, and RF on a monthly basis.

Collect and prepare/generate quality data for monthly closing presentation for Executive Meetings.

Analyze and classify each phone as valid or invalid/true bounce or non-bounce for warranty phones from Best Buy (redo), T-mobile (bounce), and AT&T (bounce).

Ability to quickly adapt to a changing environment

Strong communication skills, both verbal and written

Willingness and ability to learn new technologies

Strong technical problem solving skills

Strong critical thinking and analysis skills
