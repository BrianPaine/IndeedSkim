RESPONSIBILITIES
Magento is looking for a customer focused and service-minded individual to join our global Customer Support team.  The Support Business Analyst is someone who is flexible, assertive with an eye for detail. You are a “data forensics detective” with an ability to catch trends and seasonality. This role will be creating, updating and maintaining reports and analytics to help determine where our teams are succeeding (or where they need to improve) and identify all gaps and bottlenecks by looking at ticket and escalation trend data within the different support and engineering teams. The ultimate goal is to ensures our customer support department (as well as all supporting groups) are providing the best customer service (timeliness and quality).

Job Duties
  • Create, maintain and improve reports and dashboards in Salesforce and Zendesk for multiple teams within Customer Support.
  • Support and maintain executed metrics to track trends and performance in Salesforce/Zendesk and Power BI for our Global Support Operations.
  • Utilize programming and analytical tools including SQL and Power BI to manage data and design data models.
  • Analyze data and identify deficiencies in reporting requests with the ability to improve.
  • Present end-to-end workflow demonstrations for internal clients on created reports and analytics.
  • Collaborate with team on process improvement projects.
  • Documentation of all reports (methodology, assumption, data sources, metrics and logic)
 
Qualifications
  • BA/BS degree
  • Solid working knowledge of ticketing and CRM systems such as Salesforce and Zendesk
  • Knowledge of Power BI or other advanced BI platforms
  • Data modeling experience
  • Intermediate to advanced working knowledge of Excel and PowerPoint
  • SQL
  • Experience in working with a growing enterprise/cloud software company with thousands of customers
  • Attention to detail
  • Problem solving
  • Able to reprioritize tasks and work with competing BI demands
  • Team player
  • Self-motivated
