CarePoint Health Medical Associates Jersey City

Business Intelligence Analyst

MAIN FUNCTIONS:

  • Gather reporting requirements from requestors, share report options with the requestor.
  • Create mock-up reports for review.
  • Assure the integrity of project data, including data extraction, storage, manipulation, processing and analysis.
  • Ensure smooth and proper execution of databases used for extractions
  • Database design, security, data storage and management
  • Collect and collate appropriate data for use in databases and conduct related research.
  • Develop datasets and data that can be used for data analysis and presentation
  • Evaluation and problem investigation.
  • Analysis of information using various statistical methods, identifying patterns in data.
  • Work closely with colleagues to try and identify data related problems and identify resolutions.
  • Responsible for administration, database design, security, and maintenance.
  • Work closely with team members to develop the best practices for data repository.
  • Develop and deploy ETL packages.
  • Other duties and projects assigned.
  • Fosters a knowledge and awareness of customers business environment, work flows, their support requirements, industry standards and application systems development which improve or enhance systems

Job Requirements

EDUCATION:

  • Preferably Degree in Computer Science, Information System, Information management, Business Administration or equivalent.

EXPERIENCE:

  • 3-5 years of experience writing reports using a variety of reporting tools, SQL Reporting Services, Crystal or other reporting tools.
  • Minimum 1-3 years experience in a Healthcare Environment
  • Minimum of three years using query language (SQL or other) in complex relational databases and ability to validate reporting results
  • Excellent Written and oral communications skills
  • Minimum 2-3 years of custom reporting design and implementation experience with PS Query, SQL
  • Project Management a plus
  • Strong analytical ability
  • Knowledge of hospital health care, managed care industry and hospital information systems
  • Excellent working knowledge of data structure, relational data bases, stored procedures and views Minimum 2-3 years of custom reporting design and implementation experience with PS Query, SQL
  • Highly proficient in the use of various computer applications, Microsoft Access and Excel; practice management systems (e.g. Meditech).
  • Proficiency with data management and analysis.
  • Must be exceptionally attentive to details and follow through.
  • Demonstrated ability to understand and develop dashboard reporting.
