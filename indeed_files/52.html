Junior Data Analyst
Location: Overland Park, KS
Job Code: 207-2017K

Description

Zelis is a healthcare information technology company and market-leading provider of end-to-end healthcare claims cost management and payment solutions.

 

COMPANY BACKGROUND/CULTURE

Zelis Healthcare is an information technology company which utilizes an end-to-end technology platform to fulfill the claims cost management and payments needs of healthcare payors including large and medium-sized health plans, TPAs, Taft-Hartley Plans, providers and individuals.  The company provides a comprehensive portfolio of network management, claims integrity, payment remittance solutions and analytical services for medical, dental and workers' compensation claims to over 500 payor clients.  Additionally, the company delivers electronic payments and explanation of payments to over 200,000 healthcare providers and serves individuals with provider lookup and medical referral services.

Position Overview

The Junior Data Analyst must be able to work equally well in both technical and non-technical environments and effectively communicate technical information in a common, non-technical manner.  This individual must work well in a fast paced, demanding environment; the ability to efficiently organize and prioritize projects and assignments to meet deadlines is essential to success.  A background with healthcare IT is preferred but not required for this position.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Use internal SQL-based systems to import, process, and clean client-supplied data files in various formats
  • Use internal systems to gather and parse data from HTML documents
  • Work closely with client-facing team – requires good oral and written communication
  • Requires excellent QA skills to closely review post processed data to ensure data accuracy and integrity

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE/QUALIFICATIONS

  • Associates minimum / Bachelor’s preferred
  • Experience working with data and databases
  • Experience in MSSQL, Access, or any other relational database system.
  • Basic familiarity with HTML. Knowledge of JavaScript a plus.
