About us
Team

Linium Resources is a National Professional Recruitment firm with offices in Charlotte, NC and New York, NY, that covers several Verticals including (but not limited to) Financial Services, Information Technology, Finance / Accounting, Legal, HR, Administrative to Marketing / Sales. We are subject matter experts in relocation of professionals throughout the Country from East to West Coasts.

What makes us stand out among the best recruiting firms? It's simple: We care.

At Linium, we know there's more to making the right employment match than a formula. We also know that job searching is very stressful for both the potential employers and the applicants, especially if the latter are currently unemployed. Our goal is to make the process as seamless as possible, so you can grow confidently.

In short, we care. We care about our clients and want you to succeed. This manifests throughout our process, starting with getting to know you on a personal level. Our specializations and our reach in the markets we serve go a long way, but we also spend substantial time getting to know you. That means understanding your career goals, dream jobs or ideal applicants. Ultimately, we ensure you will be represented with your best interests at heart.

We promise to treat you with respect throughout the entire process, so you never feel as though you're left hanging or in the dark. In the end, what's best for you is what's best for all of us—recruiters and employers included.
