Job Summary

Responsible for the management of data collected in the University of Utah’s Research Databases and combining it with population level data, as well as interacting with and providing data support to researchers and study staff. Duties include developing database solutions, defining database physical structure, writing database queries and reports, new study set-up, granting database access, managing data dictionaries, creating study-specific datasets, performing database updates, and writing scripts for data imports. Generates study pedigrees for familial research.
Responsibilities

Familiar with standard concepts, practices, and procedures within a particular field. Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Performs a variety of tasks. Works under general supervision; typically reports to a project leader or manager. A certain degree of creativity and latitude is required.

Minimum Qualifications

Requires a bachelor’s degree in a related area or equivalency and 2-4 years of experience in the field or in a related area.
Preferences

Experience with SQL querying and programming language and Microsoft SQL Server experience preferred. Prior experience with and understanding of relational databases. Knowledge of reporting and query tools and practices. Experience with statistical analysis programs such as SAS or R preferred.
