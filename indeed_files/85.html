Position:  Data Analyst
Location:  Atlanta, GA
Job Id:  201698
# of Openings:  1
Position Title: Data Analyst
Department: Customer Services Group
Reports To: Manager, Data Reporting & Analysis
Salary Grade: N
FLSA Status: Exempt
Direct Reports: NA

Position Summary:
Conducts the analysis of participant and enterprise-wide data of a moderate to highly complex nature by reconciling data between multiple systems and protecting the integrity of all operational data. Leads efforts in investigating, gathering and documenting enterprise data management solutions. This includes working with business owners, executive management, and IT teams to measure, resolve, compile, and prepare the effective movement, quality, transformation and enrichment of AHA data.  Apply proven communication, analytical and problem-solving skills to fully support the enterprise in leveraging its data assets for high quality analytics solutions and reports. Prepare data sets and statistical reports to identify, quantify, and determine root-cause of data quality issues and solutions. Document and execute data validation and testing activities while providing consultative support to enterprise for data usage.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Performs data analysis to evaluate and inform business and data requirements.
  • Collect customer requirements, determine technical issues, and design reports to meet data analysis needs.
  • Identify new sources of data and methods to improve data collection, analysis, and reporting.
  • Synthesizes complex and diverse information for root-cause analysis by collecting and researching data.
  • Applies program management knowledge and experience to create projections to predict future operational needs and expected results.
  • Interprets analysis for management and assists in planning efforts to support future operations.
  • Analyze functional requirements and translate them into ETL mapping documents.
  • Execute the testing of data management solutions.
  • Provides support to the agency and individual teams with regards to data availability for reporting, researching data anomalies and/or inquires, and working with various standards to identify and resolve data-related application issues.
  • Utilize metadata repositories to support enterprise data management solutions.
  • Provides updates, status and completion information to manager and/or users, via Service Now, email, and/or in-person communication.
  • Performs all other related duties as assigned.
  • Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Strong problem-solving, organizational, and communication skills.
  • Proven ability to independently manage small to large projects.
  • Ability to write business requirements without direct supervision.
  • Ability to work collaboratively with users, developers, team leads, managers, directors, and executive management staff requiring minimal direction and oversight.
Education and Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in the fields of Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or Statistics.
  • Proficient in SQL databases, Microsoft Excel, SharePoint, and Service Now.
  • At least 3 years of experience working in a Data or Reporting Analyst role.
Working Conditions:
Generally, works in an office environment.  Must be able to sit and stand for long periods of time.  Must be able to work in a deadline-focused and fast-paced environment. Lifting not usually to exceed 10 - 20 pounds. May be required to have a valid State of Georgia driver’s license and be fully insurable for the purpose of obtaining clearance on company “Authorized Driver’s List.”  Occasional overtime as required to meet workload demands.
