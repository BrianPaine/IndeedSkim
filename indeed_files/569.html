Junior Marketing Analyst

Boathouse is looking for a Junior Marketing Analyst to become a member of the digital marketing team to develop and execute high-quality, reporting solutions across all marketing channels, as well as help with general project management. The successful candidate will have a data-driven mindset, have intermediate proficiency in Excel, and be a self-starter who can work in a fast-paced environment.

You can:

  • Aid in preparation of digital reporting for our clients across multiple sources and channels, including online advertising, social media, email, mobile, website, and more
  • Develop and communicate insights from the data to continually optimize current campaigns
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders on data collection, tracking, measurement, and reporting; ensure data is collected properly and accurately
  • Provide ad hoc statistical analyses as needed
  • Keep up with the latest industry trends in digital analytics and online technology, and share knowledge within the department

You have (Required):

  • Exceptional analytic skills with the ability to identify patterns and draw insights from multiple data sources
  • Experience in aggregating data and preparing reports
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines; ability to work independently and collaborate with other team members
  • Exceptional trouble shooting, problem solving, and research skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, with often changing priorities
  • Strong Excel skills, including pivot tables and graphing. Proficient in PowerPoint

You have (Preferred):

  • Basic knowledge of marketing and digital media tracking tools such as DoubleClick, Google Tag Manager, AdWords, etc.
  • Experience with web analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Webtrends or similar

Qualifications:

  • Minimum B.A./B.S. preferably with emphasis on marketing
  • 1-2 years professional experience
