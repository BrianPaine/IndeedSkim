Data Analyst

Information Innovators Inc. (Triple-i) is leading the convergence of information technology through innovation. We define, develop, and implement solutions to solve our federal government customers hardest problems, while saving them money and modernizing their systems. We are experts in enterprise IT services, cyber security, software and data solutions, health services, intelligence and geospatial technologies, and program management services.  At Triple-i we understand that employees make the company, and we are searching for the best and brightest to exceed our customers expectations.  If you share our passion for delivering innovative technology solutions, join the Triple-i team!

Job Description: The Deployment Health Clinical Center (DHCC) is the psychological health component of the Defense Centers of Excellence for Psychological Health and Traumatic Brain Injury (DCoE).  The Psychological Health Performance & Analytics (PHP&A) Directorate provides relevant, timely, and actionable population and programmatic psychological health data and analysis supporting evidence-based decision-making to improve the efficiency, effectiveness, and impact of the MHS psychological health system of care.

The PHP&A Directorate is seeking an applicant who can provide program analysis support in order to enhance operational effectiveness of its two divisions: the Clinical Surveillance & Medical Intelligence Division and the Program Modeling & Assessment Division.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Collect, extract, evaluate, and analyze psychological health data for the Military Health System to assess population health and clinical outcomes
  • Evaluate program functioning to assess performance against established standards/benchmarks, quality, effectiveness, and/or cost efficiencies for Service members, retirees, and their families
  • Conduct quantitative analysis on data sets and execute queries using statistical analysis software programs
  • Perform quality assurance checks on data integrity and maintain access to various data repositories
  • Develop presentations for leadership to share findings and assist in making actionable recommendations
  • Prepare analytic reports detailing findings, recommendations, and opportunities for interventions

Required Skills:
  • 3+ years of experience performing program analysis on large, complex population-level medical and health data sets
  • 3+ years of experience conducting data management and data cleaning on extremely large datasets
  • 3+ years of experience using statistical software packages to conduct descriptive and inferential statistics
  • Excellent communication skills, including verbal, written, listening, presentation, and facilitation
Desired Skills:
  • Experience writing SAS or SQL code to extract datasets from large data repositories and relational databases
  • Experience developing enhancement strategies to reduce variance and optimize performance
  • Demonstrate knowledge of military organizations and other Government agencies
Education/Certification Requirements:
  • BA or BS degree in related field of study (Master degree in Public Health or Health Informatics preferred)
Clearance Requirements:
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a ADP-II/NACLC or SECRET clearance
Location: Silver Spring, MD

Triple-i is an Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action Employer- EOE AA M/F/Vet/Disability.
