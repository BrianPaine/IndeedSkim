Data Analyst

Analytics San Francisco, California

Description


KIXEYE's looking for a high performing data analyst to work on our flagship mobile game


Do you like data? How about real-time applications used by millions of players everyday from every corner of the globe? Do you have a passion for analyzing complex business metrics and presenting your findings to key decision makers? We are currently hiring for multiple roles on our analytics team.


What you get to do every day:


  • Embed with our game team to present your analyses and findings to directly impact our games.
  • Perform a wide range of analysis to produce meaningful insights that improve engagement and monetization across our platform
  • Monitor and optimize our product performance on key dimensions for revenue and retention
  • Drive analytics product initiatives, including modeling and visualizations

What you bring to the role:


  • Experience in data analysis, gaming, mobile applications, consulting, or business/financial analysis
  • Expertise with SQL
  • Deep skills with statistical tools (e.g. Python, R, Matlab) and visualization tools (e.g. Tableau, Looker)
  • Experience with metrics driven-decision making
  • Proven experience owning business KPIs and creating presentations from these KPIs
  • Demonstrated capacity for developing and understanding business and product strategy
  • Strong communication and presentation skills
  • Demonstrated capacity for being scrappy and entrepreneurial
  • BA/BS degree required;  bonus points for STEM or Econ Fields grads and advanced degrees

Bonus:


Experience in mobile game/application analysis

Familiar with Python, Jupyter, git, predictive modeling, segmentation, etc
