EDJ Careers:
Data Analyst

Full-Time position in Louisville, KY office

Who We Are

Based in Louisville, EdjAnalytics is committed to helping clients find edge in competitive markets by leveraging their existing data in evidence-based decision making processes and complex problem solving. We find this approach can prove equally powerful across a breadth of domains from commodity traders to professional sports teams, universities to hospitals, and beyond. Our small but highly motivated team comprises dedicated individuals from diverse intellectual backgrounds, and we are encouraged to challenge each other to innovate and improve in an open, collegial work environment.

Who We Want

In an effort to provide the best services to our clients, we’re looking for a Data Analyst who will work closely with our Data Scientists to study and model client data sets; by applying both statistical and machine learning techniques to discover patterns and develop tools that aid client decision making.  As a Data Analyst, you will focus on the exploratory analysis and transforming of data to support modeling decisions and development. You will work in collaboration with other members of the technical team to facilitate the development of data mining and predictive modeling applications. Individuals in this role are expected to learn and share knowledge on a consistent basis, ultimately improving team outcomes with each new client experience.

Responsibilities

  • Explore, clean, and manipulate data
  • Perform exploratory and descriptive analytics on various data sets
  • Assist Data Scientists in the development of machine learning or statistical models
  • Create compelling data visualizations to support modeling decisions
  • Contribute as necessary to other areas of the business

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in engineering, statistics, mathematics, computer science or a related discipline emphasizing scientific inquiry
  • In-depth exposure to SQL and the analysis of data
  • Strong foundation in mathematics and/or statistics
  • Existing familiarity with one of R, Python or SAS
  • Self-drive and motivation to learn new skills and methodologies with minimal oversight
  • Flexibility and willingness to adapt in order to thrive in a collaborative startup environment
  • Proficient written and verbal communication skills
  • Must be authorized to work in the U.S.

Desired Additional Qualifications

  • Demonstrated computer programming/software development skills
  • Demonstrated experience with data visualization, particularly interactive and/or dynamic visualization
  • Experience working with groups on technical projects; experience using Github is a plus
  • Experience in communicating and presenting data and analysis to audiences with varying backgrounds
