1 month ago

# of Openings

Category

Overview

This position is on the consulting staff of a government consulting firm headquartered in Tysons, VA. Work location will be on site in client space in Scott AFB, IL.

Responsibilities

  • Provide daily support for analysis of personal property rates and performance, including rate updates, rates research, and cost comparisons based on shipment types
  • Develop reports identifying shipment volumes and types by distribution channel and code of service, and conduct peak season capacity analysis using indicators such as carrier blackouts, shipment status assessment, carrier refusal behavior, and other program leading and lagging indicators
  • Provide stakeholders with training support for using analytics interfaces and assist with developing solutions and trend analysis
  • Supports all personal property payment processes, and future electronic billing initiatives, all of which encompass the electronic billing and payment process for personal property shipments
  • Any additional ad hoc report, task, or support as needed

Qualifications

 

  • Bachelor's Degree in a related technical discipline, or the equivalent combination of education, professional training, or work experience.
  • Secret clearance is required.
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Excel, including use of Visual Basic, SQL, pivot tables, and filters.
  • 1-3 years of experience in analyzing large sets of data and presenting both graphical and tabular results.
  • Experience or knowledge of personal property and/or commercial surface transportation a plus.
  • Experience with Oracle Business Analytics solutions.
