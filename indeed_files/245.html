Data Analyst

Support (Customer & Technical) Diamond Bar, California

Apply Back to Openings

Do mounds of data excite you?  Are you good at creating a story through data and visualizations? Is machine learning and AI a passion of yours?  Liferay, like many other companies, is jumping into data, metrics and machine learning to become a more data driven business.  We are looking for like minded individuals to add and lead our team.  We are looking for someone to help us push the envelope in data collection, manipulation and reporting.

<p

Responsibilities

    • Translate business needs and questions into reports and visualizations so that stakeholders can make data driven decisions
    • Impact change by testing and supporting hypotheses through data mining tools
    • Bring stakeholders confidence by continuing to build and maintain reporting tools and recurring reports
  • Protect data to ensure data integrity and to make it consistent and reusable
  • Influence data collection methods and best practices
  • Support and troubleshoot issues with reports and distribution of those reports
  • Create datasets to run through machine learning
  • Maintain and validate machine learning
<p

Qualifications

  • B.S. in Mathematics, Statistics or related field
  • Experience with IT and database systems
  • Experience with reporting tool Tableau, MySQL and Excel
  • Good understanding of relational and dimensional data models
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects at one time
  • Pay close attention to detail, be resourceful and deliver results
