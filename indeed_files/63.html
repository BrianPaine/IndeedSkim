Data Analyst

Location: Burlington, NJ
Address:
Zip Code: 08016
Requisition ID: 5501813
Posting Date: 09/08/2017
Shift: Day Shift
Status:
Share
Bring your passion for fashion to today's Burlington Stores Inc. If you are interested in a career in retail, consider joining one of the largest off price retailers of apparel and home furnishings in the nation. We always have a large selection of quality name brand merchandise at huge savings; coats, clothing and shoes for the whole family, fashionable accessories, home decor, and everything for baby. Burlington means one-stop shopping for labels you love at prices you love even more. With more than 600 stores, we're always looking for good talent that can drive results.

The Data Analyst, reporting to the Director of Sourcing will initially be focused on assisting with building of a standardized reporting processes by utilizing historical spend data to actively analyze and report spend coverage based on actual spend. Once the initial process is built the Analyst will be responsible for managing the spend coverage analysis process, managing and distributing the weekly project update report, and assisting the Procurement team with analytical support to drive projects and develop strategies based on the outputs.
 
Major Duties and Responsibilities:
Spend Coverage Reporting
• Assist in the development of a reporting tool to capture the progress of the Procurement team’s as a means to measure departmental effectiveness
• Cleanse, normalize, categorize and cross-reference large files of raw indirect spend data, originating from multiple sources, to use in reporting, auditing and strategy development
• Own and manage the historical spend database ensuring accuracy, completeness, and the availability of data
Approved Vendor List Management
• Actively own and manage the list of approved vendors by reviewing contracts, pricing agreements, spend data and by working with other Procurement team and business unit members to understand which vendors are approved and identify unapproved spend
Project Reporting
• Manage the project tracker report and the procurement project status report
• Ensure that the savings reporting field in each project is accurate and has gone through the approval process prior to posting as “complete”
Analytical Support
• Provide analytical support to the Procurement team as needed which may include:
• Spend data analysis
• Market analysis
• Sourcing analysis
• Contract data management
• General analytical support
• Provide strategic data analysis insights to drive Procurement and/or company strategy decisions


Education
Four (4) year degree or five plus (5) years of experience in a similar role required; degree with an emphasis in Mathematics, Finance, Supply Chain, or MIS preferred
Skills and Abilities
• Strong analytical skills and ability to identify key trends, root causes, and the ability to draw conclusions and propose solutions based on the evidence
• Advanced MS Excel capabilities.
• Proficiency in MS Access.
• Demonstrated proficiency in managing and manipulating very large data sets through Excel and/or Access.


Our ideal candidates will be self-motivated, team players, who have the desire to succeed. We care about our employees, have a supportive environment that values trust and respect , offer a competitive wage and benefits package that includes a generous paid time off plan, plus an associate discount, and a company matched 401(k) to name a few.
