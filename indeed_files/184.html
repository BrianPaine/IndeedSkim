Job Summary

Scope of Responsibility: The MHAC Data Analyst / Liaison works closely with MHAC leadership, MHAC team members, and representatives from all markets served to insure data capture and integrity is maintained and reported in a manner promoting / enhancing the strategic vision of each client  / market served by the MHAC and its services.  

Job Summary:

The Data Analyst / Liaison is responsible for insuring data accuracy and integrity are maintained in all MHAC databases and submits timely and accurate reports as identified and requested by each market and / or Mercy leadership.  As a member of the MHAC team, this position will assist in the training and orientation of MHAC team members / end users on system functionality to ensure timeliness, completeness, consistency, and integrity of data; and will work with leadership to identify efficient and effective ways to manage all databases and workflows related to data capture. 

Effectively retrieves, compiles and presents data from various sources in support of MHAC and / or market functions promoting the overall strategic vision of the operation and its customers 
Serves as the primary liaison with MHAC customers related to business development and data requests 
Analyzes data and establishes facts with valid conclusions utilizing various database applications  
Generates daily, weekly, monthly volume analysis reports for leadership and customers.
Works closely with customers to establish referral trends and business strategies to maintain / grow market share relative to MHAC operations 
Works with customers to develop relationships with physicians, referrals sites, and others by leveraging MHAC operations and data analysis 
Communicates market intel back to MHAC leadership to support operational efficiency and effectiveness
Responsible for other tasks and analysis as requested / directed 

Required Minimum Education: 4 yr Bachelors Degree

Preferred Education: Graduate Degree (Masters)
