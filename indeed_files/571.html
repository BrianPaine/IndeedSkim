Data Analyst
65301BR
OVERVIEW
Support our client-dedicated Integrated Facilities Management team through involvement in business systems tied to financial, work order data, facility statistics, etc.
 
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
  • Preparation and analysis of regular and ad-hoc reports
  • Run day to day reporting and forecasting activities
  • Monthly, quarterly & annual report generation
  • Maintain and enhance reporting
  • Complete and support regular and ad-hoc reporting including informal and formal internal and documents, reports, graphs, charts and presentations.
  • Organize and maintain team data collection, reporting and communication.
  • Support and implement “Best in Class” reporting.
  • Support team’s efforts to develop efficient reporting strategies including automation and simplification of reporting processes while preserving data integrity
  • Support operational analysis and performance to ensure flawless execution against KPIs and other associated metrics
  • Work closely with and support Sourcing Group in implementing reporting routines to ensure seamless and timely service delivery 
  • Develop and maintain positive client relationship
  • Conflict resolution and solution analysis
  • Support cost-savings and process improvement initiatives
  • Participate in the development and distribution of best practices, process documentation, and user support materials for reporting.
  • Identify tactical and strategic opportunities, gaps and financial risks through collaboration with cross-functional teams.
  • Analyze performance data to forecast/trend.
  • Support finance with reactive and adaptive budget reporting
  • Build transparency of Supplier spend and key risks related to our Suppliers. Lead reporting of results and opportunities.
  • Administer and maintain Supplier contract files to ensure compliancy and all necessary contract documentation requirements.
  • Ensure compliance to Sourcing and contracting policies and procedures.
  • Manage Supplier scorecards, contract compliance and service obligations.
  • Support Sourcing Managers in standardized RFP/bid and contract processes.
  • Ability to innovate new programs or processes that will benefit our Client and Jones Lang LaSalle.
  • Any and all other duties and tasks assigned
 
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE
  • Minimum of 5 years of work experience with data analysis or database management
  • Minimum 5 years of work experience with Microsoft Business Intelligence stack (SharePoint, PowerPivot, PowerView, SSRS)
 
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES
  • Strong SQL, data cleansing, data integration skills
  • Excellent PC skills, proficient in MS applications
  • Very strong in Microsoft Excel, Word & PowerPoint (Excel super user preferred)
  • Excellent Inter-personal skills
  • Organization skills
  • Strong analytical ability
  • Self-motivated, able to set priorities and work autonomously
  • Results driven – ability to analyze, act, and implement to achieve desired results
  • Outstanding customer service skills
  • Ability to translate innovative ideas to help our teams and clients work smarter
  • Proven ability to deliver results in challenging environments
  • Motivated to grow and perform in a high performance organization
  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment with strict timelines.
  • May solve highly technical complex problems and be called on to consult for other projects.

About JLL

JLL (NYSE: JLL) is a leading professional services firm that specializes in real estate and investment management. A Fortune 500 company, JLL helps real estate owners, occupiers and investors achieve their business ambitions. In 2016, JLL had revenue of $6.8 billion and fee revenue of $5.8 billion and, on behalf of clients, managed 4.4 billion square feet, or 409 million square meters, and completed sales acquisitions and finance transactions of approximately $145 billion. At the end of the second quarter of 2017, JLL had nearly 300 corporate offices, operations in over 80 countries and a global workforce of more than 80,000. As of June 30, 2017, LaSalle Investment Management had $57.6 billion of real estate under asset management. JLL is the brand name, and a registered trademark, of Jones Lang LaSalle Incorporated. For further information, visit www.jll.com.
